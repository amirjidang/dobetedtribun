Cristiano Ronaldos Age, Height, Weight, And Measurements

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 19, 2024

Known by his nicknames “CR7” and “Cristiano,” he is a Portuguese professional football player for Real Madrid of Spain and the Portugal national team.

Christiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro are a global sports icon and one of the most recognized names in professional football.

Cristiano Ronaldo, a world-famous soccer player, was born in the Portuguese Madeira, located a couple of hundred kilometers south of Portugal.

Youngest son of municipal gardener parents Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro and Jose Dinis Aveiro, Ronaldo was born in Santo Antonio. Hugo Ronaldo, Elma, and Liliana Cátia, Ronaldo’s older sisters, are also very important to him.

Read to know about Cristiano Ronaldo’s age, height, weight, and measurements;

Cristiano Ronaldo Age

As of 2022, He is 37 years old.

Dos Santos Ronaldo, Cristiano Aveiro began playing football/soccer at eight with the amateur team from his hometown, Andorinha. Soon after his tenth birthday, CR7 moved to CD Nacional, one of the most successful clubs in Madeira. 

Shortly after that, in 1996, Cristiano Ronaldo was persuaded to join Sporting Lisbon, one of Portugal’s most prestigious clubs.

The world took notice of Cristiano Ronaldo when he was just 17 years old. His excellent play for Portugal’s youth team in the UEFA Under 17 Championship solidified his status as one of the sport’s brightest young talents.

Liverpool and Arsenal were among the numerous elite teams represented by scouts at the competition.

Manchester United paid a then-record 12 million pounds to a player so young in 2003. There’s been no turning back now.

Ronaldo was only the fourth footballer to have a wax figure at London’s Madame Tussauds in June 2010. Besides being named the UEFA Club Forward of the Year, Ronaldo has won the Ballon d’Or, the Golden Boot, and FIFA World Player.

Ronaldo also owns a clothing store for men called CR7 and models the label’s wares. Diamond-encrusted garments are the store’s claim to fame.

Despite his fame and fortune, he constantly gives back to the community. He is an ambassador for Save the Children and the Mangrove Care Forum in Indonesia and has helped support schools in Gaza.

Cristiano Ronaldo’s Height, Weight, And Measurements

Ronaldo stands at a towering 6 feet, 1 inch (185 cm) tall, and weighs a svelte 83 kilograms (179 lb.). He has a size 10 foot (US); the measurements for Cristiano Ronaldo are 43 inches tall, 33 inches wide, and 14 inches in circumference.

He has a 43-inch chest, a 33-inch waist, and 14-inch biceps. Ronaldo, a player for Real Madrid, has black hair and hazel-brown eyes.
