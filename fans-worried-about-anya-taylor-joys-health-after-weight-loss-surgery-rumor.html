Fans Worried About Anya Taylor-Joy's Health After She Was Accused Of Getting Weight Loss Surgery

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Highlights

  • Anya Taylor-Joy's changing appearance sparked concern and rumors of plastic surgery among fans.
  • Fans noticed a significant difference in Taylor-Joy's cheeks, suspecting buccal fat removal surgery.
  • Concerns were raised when Taylor-Joy wore a corset, impacting views on unhealthy beauty standards.

Over the last several years, several stars have revealed the health issues they are coping with. Some examples of that include Tom Hanks' diabetes and staph infection, Lucy Hale's addictions, and Busy Phillipps who revealed she has several health issues.

Unlike those stars who've spoken out about their health, Anya Taylor-Joy's fans are the ones who've expressed concerns about her. Especially after rumors started that she underwent plastic surgery.

This article will start by looking at why some fans have becomeworried about Taylor-Joy's health. Following that, this article will look at why some people have become concerned about how Taylor-Joy's physical transformation may affect young women.

Fans Believe Anya Taylor-Joy Got Weight Loss Surgery

After Anya Taylor-Joy starred in the movie Split, she became a world-famous actor. When somebody reaches that kind of stature, they inevitably get the superstar treatment, which includes having their photo taken endlessly. After comparing photos of Taylor-Joy over time, many fans concluded that the actor may have undergone plastic surgery. Specifically, many observers believe that Taylor-Joy has gotten buccal fat removal surgery.

Over the last several years, it has seemed like buccal fat removal surgery has become very popular among celebrities.

According to Johns Hopkins Medicine's website, undergoing the procedure changes how a person's cheeks look. More specifically, the surgery results in people no longer having round cheeks.

"Buccal fat removal is an elective plastic surgery procedure to remove fat in the lower cheek, which gives the bottom part of the face a narrower shape and emphasizes the cheekbones and jawline."

Johns Hopkins Medicine continues, "Buccal fat is a pad of adipose tissue (fat) in the cheek below each cheekbone. The size and shape of these pads varies from person to person. More fat in the buccal region can give the face a round appearance."

Johns Hopkins Medicine continues, "Removing buccal fat can make the cheekbones more defined and by creating a shadow contrast between the cheekbones and the lower jaw while making the face look less round and more V-shaped."

While fans have worried about Anya Taylor-Joy's changing face, some in the fashion business clearly feel differently. After all, in 2021, Taylor-Joy won the Face of the Year award at the CFDA Fashion Awards.

Comparing newer and older photos of Taylor-Joy, there is no doubt that the actor's cheeks look very different. More specifically, Taylor-Joy's face has changed in ways that match the description of the effects of buccal fat removal surgery.

Alternatively, some observers have questioned if Taylor-Joy's changing face has been caused by nothing more than weight loss. That certainly seems possible too, since many celebrities lose weight due to the unhealthy beauty standards famous women are held to.

Either way, many of Taylor-Joy's fans have become very concerned about her health. Those feelings were caused by more than just the actor's changing face.

In February 2024, a photo of Taylor-Joy wearing a corset went viral. That photo showed the world how incredibly thin Taylor-Joy's waist looked while wearing the old-fashioned garment, much to the shock of many.

To say that the corset photo got a big response is a huge understatement. In many cases, the responding fans expressed a great deal of concern for the actor's health. One example of that is the fact that one social media user expressed concern about how the corset affected Taylor-Joy's body.

“Are your ribs okay? Could you breathe? Like, honestly wondering!”

Anya Taylor-Joy's Corset Photo Inspired A Harsh Backlash

When the photo of Anya Taylor-Joy wearing a corset went viral, some observers expressed concern for her. On the other hand, several observers expressed their anger at Taylor-Joy and how the corset photo may affect young women who observe it.

In February 2024, a writer for The Daily Mail named Tanith Carey tackled how Taylor-Joy's corset photos may affect teen girls. A writer who is focused on parenting books, Carey cited her years of extensive research.

"In reality the image is incredibly dangerous – especially to impressionable young girls. As an author of ten parenting books, I have interviewed schoolgirls who wear waist-trainers under their school uniforms in a desperate effort to whittle down their waists."

Carey later continued to explain the real danger of the photo as she saw it. As Carey's piece explains, she worries that the biggest danger of the corset photo is it could inspire some people to go to extremely dangerous lengths to lose weight.

Just like Carey, several social media users expressed their worries about the example Taylor-Joy's corset photo set for impressionable young women. One perfect example of that is the fact that one woman wrote, “Can we not normalize starvation?” on social media.

Another social media user expressed their opinion that Taylor-Joy's corset photo is damaging to society. “Do you realize how corsets are hurtful for women’s health and should not be promoted?”
