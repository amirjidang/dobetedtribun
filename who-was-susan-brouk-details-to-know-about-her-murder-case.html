Who Was Susan Brouk? Details To Know About Her Murder Case

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Susan Brouk was murdered in 1998 along with her children that sparked rage in Missouri. Here are complete details on the murder case!

Susan Brouk was a Missouri woman whose murder sparked one of the few death penalties to its perpetrator.

Along with her children, Adrian and Kyle, Brouk was also killed, and their dead bodies were thrown in a pond.

Mark Christeson and his cousin caused this brutal homicide.

While Mark faces the death penalty, his cousin still serves life imprisonment without parole.

Susan Brouk Murder – Who Killed Her?

Susan Brouk’s murder was committed by teenagers Mark Christeson and his cousin, Jesse Carter.

The two had entered Brouk’s house to steal her Ford Bronco.

In the process of their crime commitment, Christeson raped the mother of two before he killed her.

Christeson was treated as the main perpetrator as he had raped Brouk, killed her children, dumped them in the pond, and stole things from her house.

The incident happened in 1998 that heated the Missouri law enforcement.

Susan Brouk Cause of Death Revealed

Susan Brouk’s main cause of death is revealed to be the slashing of her throat.

She and her son’s throat were slashed while her daughter was suffocated and drowned in the pond.

Christeson and Carter opted to kill the family of three after Brouk’s daughter recognized Carter and said his name.

According to Daily Mail UK, Christeson kicked Brouk in the ribs and cut her throat, then cut Kyle’s throat and held him underwater until he drowned. 

Carter held Adrian while Christeson pressed on her throat until she suffocated, and then Carter pushed the girl’s body into the pond.

As Brouk struggled to stay alive, the cousins tossed her into the pond to drown.

Meet Susan Brouk Family

Susan Brouk lived with her small family consisting of her 12-year-old daughter, Adrian, and a 9-year-old son, Kyle.
