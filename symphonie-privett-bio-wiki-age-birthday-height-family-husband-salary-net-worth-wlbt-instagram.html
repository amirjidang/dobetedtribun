Symphonie Privett Biography and Wiki

Symphonie Privett is an American journalist who began her career at KAIT in Jonesboro, Arkansas. She currently works as a Morning News Anchor for WLBT in Jackson, Mississippi, U.S.

Before joining the WLBT team as an anchor in December 2017, Symphonie Privett began her professional career at KAIT as an associate producer for Good Morning Region 8 and later she began working as MMJ for KAIT’s evening news shows.

While there, Symphonie won two Arkansas Associated Press Broadcasters awards on her pieces highlighting organ donation and young women beating the odds, while living with rare genetic disorders. Nevertheless, in 2015, Privett began anchoring for KAIT’s morning news show good morning Region 8. Moreover, she anchored the KAIT’s evening news shows; covering breaking news, crime, severe weather, local government, education, among other beats.
