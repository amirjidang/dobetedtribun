This article will list the top eight highest-grossing films with Lady Gaga based on how much money they brought in at global box offices together. Whether you have been a fan of Gaga for a long time or are just interested in learning more about her acting career, this list will give you an idea of which of her films have been the most highly received by viewers.

Sin City: A Dame to Kill For (2014) - $39.4 million

Lady Gaga briefly appears as a waitress at a casino in the 2014 neo-noir action thriller flick. Although her role in the movie was limited, her appearance was a highlight for many of her fans. Despite a star-studded cast including Jessica Alba and Joseph Gordon-Levitt, the picture underperformed at the box office, collecting only $39.4 million worldwide. Lady Gaga's brief but remarkable presence in the movie remains unforgettable.

Muppets Most Wanted (2014) - $80.4 million

Lady Gaga makes a brief cameo appearance in the movie as herself. She sings and dances with the Muppets. Even though her part in the film was small, she still got to sing and dance, which helped make the scene stand out. Despite having mixed reviews, Muppets Most Wanted grossed $80.4 million worldwide, making it a moderate success. House of Gucci (2021) - $240.5 million

Via Flickr/Cartoon Lovers

The murder drama movie House of Gucci was released in 2021. The primary character of Patrizia Reggiani, who is being convicted of organizing the murder of her ex-husband Maurizio Gucci, is played by Lady Gaga. Reggiani was found guilty of the crime. The film, helmed by Ridley Scott and starring Adam Driver and Jared Leto, has amassed an astonishing total of $240.5 million in revenue across the globe of March 2022. Critics have given the movie varying ratings, although everyone has applauded Lady Gaga's performance.

Hotel Transylvania (2012) - $358.4 million

Via Flickr/Thefanboyseo1

In 2012, Lady Gaga voiced Mavis in the animated family picture Hotel Transylvania. The film's subject is the story of Dracula (voiced by Adam Sandler) and his daughter Mavis, who own a hotel for monsters. Hotel Transylvania was a cinema office hit that made $358.4 million worldwide and inspired two sequels. Even though Gaga played a minor role in the picture, her presence increased its charm and appeal, making it popular with viewers of all ages. It's easy to argue that Hotel Transylvania significantly influenced Gaga's ascent to fame in Hollywood.
