These Are Orlando Bloom's Famous Ex-Girlfriends

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Hollywood star Orlando Bloom and singer Katy Perry have been together since January 2016 and in February 2019 the two stars got engaged. In August 2020, Katy Perry gave birth to their daughter Daisy Dove Bloom. The couple has an impressive joint net worth of $370 million, and six years later they seem as happy as ever, even though they had some ups and downs.

Before Orlando Bloom started dating the pop star, he was linked to numerous other famous women. From Miranda Kerr to Lindsay Lohan — keep on scrolling to find out just whom the actor was with before Katy Perry!

8 Miranda Kerr Was Married To Orlando Bloom From 2010 Until 2014

Kicking the list off is Australian model Miranda Kerr. Bloom and Kerr started dating in November 2007, and in June 2010 the couple got engaged. A month later the couple tied the knot, unfortunately in October 2013, they announced their separation. Their divorce was finalized in early 2014. Together, Orlando Bloom and Miranda Kerr have a son named Flynn Christopher Blanchard Copeland Bloom who was born in January 2011. The Australian model rose to fame in 2007 as a Victoria's Secret Angel, and since then she's worked with brands like Dior, Chanel, John Galliano, and many more. Luckily for Orlando Bloom, his current fiancée Katy Perry and his ex-wife Miranda Kerr seem to be friends, and they only say nice things about each other.

7 Lindsay Lohan Was Linked To Orlando Bloom In 2006

Next up is actress Lindsay Lohan who was, according to Who's Dated Who, linked to Orlando Bloom back in 2006. The actress rose to fame in 1998 thanks to starring in the Disney family movie The Parent Trap, and in the early 2000s, she was one of the biggest teen stars thanks to roles in movies like Mean Girls, Freaky Friday, and Confessions of a Teenage Drama Queen.

In 2006 Lohan was still at the peak of her fame, and Orlando Bloom was already known for starring in projects like The Lord of the Rings movies and the Pirates of the Caribbean franchise.

6 Liv Tyler Was Linked To Orlando Bloom In 2013

Let's move on to Hollywood star Liv Tyler, who was linked to Orlando Bloom in 2013, after his separation from Miranda Kerr. As fans know, Bloom and Tyler know each other from The Lord of the Rings franchise in which he portrays Legolas while she plays Arwen Undómiel. Liv Tyler rose to fame in the mid-90s with roles in projects like Empire Records, That Thing You Do!, and Stealing Beauty.

5 Kate Bosworth Dated Orlando Bloom From 2002 Until 2006

Actress Kate Bosworth is next on today's list. According to Who's Dated Who, Bosworth dated Orlando Bloom between 2002 and 2006. The actress rose to fame in the early 2000s with roles in projects like Blue Crush, Wonderland, and Beyond the Sea.

4 Vanessa Lachey Dated Orlando Bloom In 2005

Next on the list is actress and host Vanessa Lachey who was at the time known as Vanessa Minnillo as she hadn't married singer Nick Lachey yet. According to Who's Dated Who, Vanessa Minnillo dated Orlando Bloom between April and August 2005.

Vanessa Lachey rose to fame as a host of Total Request Live on MTV in 2003, and since then she has worked on projects like NCIS: Hawaiʻi, Love Is Blind, and Entertainment Tonight.

3 Helena Christensen Was Linked To Orlando Bloom In 2003

Let's move on to Danish supermodel Helena Christensen. According to Who's Dated Who, Christensen was reportedly linked to Orlando Bloom between August and September 2003. Helena Christensen rose to fame in the early '90 thanks to starring in the music video for Chris Isaak's song "Wicked Game". Since then, she has been known as a Victoria's Secret Angel, and over the course of her career, she has worked with famous brands like Chanel, Versace, Prada, and many more.

2 Sienna Miller Dated Orlando Bloom In 2001

Actress Sienna Miller is next on today's list. According to Who's Dated Who, Miller and Bloom dated between January and May 2001. Sienna Miller rose to fame in the early 2000s with roles in movies like Layer Cake and Alfie. Since then, the actress has starred in numerous famous projects such as The Girl, Mississippi Grind, The Lost City of Z, and many more.

1 Ashley Haas Was Linked To Orlando Bloom In 2017

And lastly, wrapping the list up is model Ashley Haas. According to Who's Dated Who, Haas was linked to Orlando Bloom in 2017. In March 2017, Orlando Bloom and Katy Perry called it quits before getting back together in late 2017. Ashley Haas is signed with WME & Elite Model Management, and apart from modeling, she has also appeared in the movies American Fango and Cicada.
