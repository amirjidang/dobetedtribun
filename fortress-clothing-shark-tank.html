About Fortress Clothing and Its Founder

Fortress Clothing, founded by Dale Lewis in 2011, specializes in producing outdoor athletic gear designed to withstand extreme temperatures. Their products, including base layers, trousers, jackets, and gloves, are equipped with their patented Aeries insulation technology. This technology ensures that wearers stay warm even when the clothes are wet, making them ideal for harsh weather conditions.

Dale Lewis, the founder of Fortress Clothing, is a business and marketing graduate from the University of Utah. Prior to starting Fortress Clothing, he owned a successful telecommunication company. Inspired by his passion for outdoor activities and his desire to create gear that could withstand any climate, Lewis launched Fortress Clothing in 2011.

Fortress Clothing’s Shark Tank Experience

When Fortress Clothing appeared on Shark Tank, founder Dale Lewis had high hopes of securing a $600,000 investment for 15% equity in the company. During the pitch, Lewis impressed the panel of sharks by showcasing the effectiveness of Fortress Clothing’s extreme weather gear. In a captivating moment, he invited Robert Herjavec to test the product inside a cryo chamber, highlighting its exceptional insulation properties.

Although the product wowed the sharks, no deal was made. Lori Greiner, Barbara Corcoran, Kevin O’Leary, and Mark Cuban expressed concerns about the company’s market focus and the absence of a clear marketing strategy. Despite not securing an investment, Fortress Clothing emerged from the Shark Tank experience with heightened attention and a surge in sales.

Since its appearance on Shark Tank, Fortress Clothing has achieved remarkable sales success, surpassing $3.5 million in revenue since 2012. This demonstrates the market’s recognition of the quality and effectiveness of their extreme weather gear.

    Fortress Clothing’s Founder, Dale Lewis

    Before starting Fortress Clothing, Dale Lewis had a successful career in the telecommunication industry. In fact, his telecommunication company was sold for an impressive $33 million, allowing him to retire at the age of 40. However, Lewis’s retirement didn’t last long, as his passion for outdoor activities and entrepreneurial spirit led him to embark on a new journey.

    In 2011, Lewis decided to launch Fortress Clothing, a company specializing in extreme weather apparel. Inspired by his own experiences navigating harsh climates, Lewis saw an opportunity to create innovative gear that could withstand any environment.

    While getting Fortress Clothing off the ground was no easy task, Lewis’s determination paid off. In 2017, the company launched a successful Kickstarter campaign, raising $46,264 to bring their high-performance clothing to a wider audience.

    Through his bold decision to venture into the world of entrepreneurship and his commitment to creating top-notch outdoor gear, Dale Lewis has cemented himself as a respected figure in the industry. His experience in the telecommunication sector undoubtedly contributed to his business acumen, enabling him to navigate the challenges of building a successful brand like Fortress Clothing.
