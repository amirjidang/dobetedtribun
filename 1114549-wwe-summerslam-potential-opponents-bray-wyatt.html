WWE SummerSlam 2019: 5 Potential Opponents For The Fiend Bray Wyatt

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 17, 2024

It’s always great when there’s something fresh, new and exciting in the WWE product. More often than not, staleness can take over, but Bray Wyatt has proven over the years to be anything but stale. His new character debuted around late April, making an on-screen return for the first time since August 2018. It was an extended hiatus for Wyatt, but he returned with the Firefly Fun House, something that turned heads and received acclaim for its dark undertones and crass sense of humor.

WWE waited weeks to build up to Wyatt’s re-debut and when it came, it took the wrestling world by storm. Having the fiend-ish mask strapped on, Wyatt looked creepier and scarier than he ever had before, taking out Finn Balor. The commentators mentioned that “Wyatt is out to terrorize WWE”, which means that there are going to be multiple targets over the coming period of time.

It looks like WWE has tapped into something very special and Wyatt’s first match in a year could be against any top star, heel or face. Here are five good options.
