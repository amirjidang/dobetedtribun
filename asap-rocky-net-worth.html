A$AP Rocky's Net Worth Doubled, Thanks To Rihanna And Fatherhood

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Highlights

  • A$AP Rocky's net worth has doubled as of early 2024, and he has transformed into a devoted family man thanks to his partner Rihanna and their two children.
  • A$AP Rocky's legal troubles and past incidents of misconduct have overshadowed his personal growth as a father.
  • Rihanna's influence and financial wisdom are believed to have contributed to A$AP Rocky's increased net worth and career success.

Rakim Athelaston Mayers, best known as A$AP Rocky, was once a spoiled kid who often found himself enmired in trouble.

Thanks to his partner and love of his life, Rihanna – and with the arrival of their now 20-month-old son (RZA Athelston Mayers, named to honor Wu-Tang Clan member RZA), and four-month-old daughter, Riot Rose Mayers – A$AP Rocky is now a devoted family man.

On April 20th 2022 – less than a month before Rihanna and A$AP Rocky's first child, RZA, was born on May 13th 2022 – A$AP Rocky was in jail, arrested for allegedly shooting former childhood friend Terrel Ephron (also known as A$AP Relli) in November 2021.

Ephron asserted that he and Rocky were involved in a heated argument, and that A$AP withdrew a handgun, firing multiple shots, one of which grazed his fingers.

Although Rocky was released on bond after spending three hours in jail, after this incident, there were few public feuds or controversies involving Mayers following the birth of his first child.

But this charge has been like a thorn in A$AP Rocky's side; in November 2023, Los Angeles County Superior Court Judge M.L. Villar ruled that "sufficient evidence [existed] to allow the case against Mayers to proceed to trial."

If found guilty, he could face at least nine years in prison. In November 2023, Relli testified, claiming the incident was an "ambush," adding:

"But I would never be scared of Rocky ... We knew each other for years. I'm not scared of him."

That incident was not the first time A$AP Rocky found himself in trouble. He was arrested in 2019 after a sprawling altercation in Stockholm, Sweden.

In court, he asserted he had acted in the interests of self-defense, but he was found guilty, and he faced one month in a detention center.

In 2015, a brawl with his manager became news, and in 2013, he made headlines for allegedly slapping a woman at a music festival in Sydney, Australia.

However, the alleged victim of the music festival incident (Lisamarie Wade) failed to appear in court for a hearing, electing to file a subsequent lawsuit – which was settled out of court in 2015.
