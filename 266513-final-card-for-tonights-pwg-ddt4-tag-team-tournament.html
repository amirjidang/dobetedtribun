Final Card for Tonights PWG DDT4 Tag Team Tournament

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024

Tonight's Pro Wrestling Guerrilla DDT4 tag team tournament is live from the American Legion in Reseda, CA at 8pm. Below is the final card, and Wrestlezone will have results from tonight's event as soon as they become available. 

1. The Briscoe Brothers vs. Willie Mack & "Mr. Wrestling" Kevin Steen 

2. Eddie Edwards & Roderick Strong vs. Adam Cole & Kyle O'Reilly

3. Ricochet & Rich Swann vs. The Young Bucks

4. Brian Cage & Michael Elgin vs. Super Smash Bros. 


Sami Callihan vs. Drake Younger 

For more information, visit ProWrestlingGuerrilla.com!
