Dolce &amp; Gabbana Spoiled Kourtney Kardashian During Her Italian Wedding

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Dolce & Gabbana gave Kourtney Kardashian the royal treatment when she married Travis Barker in Italy last year.

The couple’s new Hulu special ‘Til Death Do Us Part goes into detail about their days-long wedding. Kourtney was invited by Dolce & Gabana to stay in the brand’s private estate in Portofino, Italy. While there, the reality star was able to hand-pick her and her family’s looks for the wedding from the designer brand’s '90s archive collection.

Kourtney sported a number of memorable looks during her Italian wedding celebration. For the ceremony, Kourtney went untraditional in a black, corseted mini dress, while Travis was chic in an all-black suit. Both of their outfits were designed by Dolce & Gabbana.

But Kourtney’s Italian wedding wasn’t the only time she wore Dolce & Gabbana to celebrate her nuptials. A month before traveling to Italy, Travis and Kourtney legally tied the knot in a courthouse ceremony in Santa Barbara.

For the event, the reality star rocked a white, corset-style mini dress from Dolce & Gabbana, which apparently retails for $1,900. Compared to the dress Kim Kardashian wore during her wedding to Kanye West, which cost over $500,000, Kourtney’s choice was surprisingly inexpensive.

Kourtney’s dress choice may have been cheaper than her sister’s, but her wedding celebration was still a lavish affair. It’s been estimated that Travis and Kourtney spent at least $3 million on the Italian festivities alone.

The couple apparently splurged to transport their guests to Italy via private jet (they borrowed Kim and Kylie’s jets to do so), though it can still cost nearly $1 million to do so considering the number of guests the pair brought along.

While Travis and Kourtney stayed at Dolce & Gabbana’s luxury estate, they couple booked rooms for their guests at Splendido hotel, which offers rooms for around $6,000 per night.

The couple also rented out Castello Brown, a historic castle, for a weekend to host the ceremony and reception. They would’ve also incurred big bills from catering, open bar, flowers, and other typical wedding costs.

One atypical thing Kourtney and Travis had at their wedding was a private concert from Andrea Bocelli, which can cost anywhere between $500,000 to $749,000. They likely would’ve needed to cover the singer’s travel expenses, too.

Later in the festivities, the couple had DJ Cassidy perform exclusively for guests, which costs between $40,000 to $74,999.

Needless to say, Travis and Kourtney’s wedding was a lavish event, and not only because Dolce & Gabbana were sponsoring it.
