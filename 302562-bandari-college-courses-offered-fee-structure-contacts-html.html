Bandari College courses offered, fee structure, contacts

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

It is that time of the year again when you need to make a wise decision on which career path to follow. A lot of options are probably flashing in your mind on what to settle for. Worry no more! Bandari College has got you covered with the various courses offered under marine, operations, technical and management unit.

This college was founded in the year 1980 mainly for training Kenya Ports Authority staff members. It now provides training needs in the maritime sector. Here is Bandari College courses, fees and contacts.

Bandari College courses

Bandari College courses are subdivided into four units:

  • Technical Training Unit
  • Operations Training Unit
  • Marine Training Unit
  • Management Training Unit
