What Married At First Sight's Iris Caldwell's Life Looks Like Today

Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Married At First Sight is an unusual dating show, but it is still nowhere close to the strangest reality dating shows from the 2000s. Iris Caldwell was on the ninth season of Married At First Sight for a chance to clinch a life partner. In it, Iris was married to Keith Manley for two months. Fans of the show had high hopes for the couple, but Keith’s decision to end the marriage failed to rank them amongst reality TV couples who made it work.

Iris may not have gotten her happily ever after from the show, but she gained the necessary insights to navigate future relationships. In March, she shared her opinions on the season 14 MAFS couples with People. Iris has been living her best life since leaving the show. The MAFS alum has been booked and busy with endorsements, sponsorships, travel, modeling, and speaking engagements.

8 What Iris Caldwell Has Been Doing Since Married At First Sight

Before joining the ninth season of the Married At First Sight show, Iris Caldwell combined her job as a youth programs coordinator of a non-profit organization with modeling. Per her website, the reality TV alum is no stranger to modeling; her modeling profile is rich with over ten years of experience. With the exposure she got from the show, the North Carolina native has been pushing her modeling career to full light. Iris's job as a model does not stop at walking the runway, she also offers modeling consultation services to brands and individuals.

7 Is Iris Caldwell Still A Virgin?

Iris Caldwell is still saving herself for marriage. Iris’s virginity became the focal point of her marriage with Keith on the MAFS show. Before the big reveal, the reality TV star was silent about that part of her life, causing fans to wonder what secret she was hiding. Iris left the show with her ‘precious gift’ after Keith called off their marriage on decision day. On "The Love Hour" podcast, Iris revealed that her decision to remain a virgin was based on her convictions.

6 Iris Caldwell Is A YouTuber

Iris is one celeb with an amazing YouTube channel. The Married At First Sight alum is fun, energetic, and outgoing, and there is no better way of showcasing these qualities than on the video streaming application. Iris’ channel is majorly about her lifestyle and features natural hair tutorials, travel vlogs, wig reviews, and fashion brand hauls.

Iris’s videos are tailored to make her audiences smile, as the reality TV star includes some humor in her content. Although not consistent with posting on the platform, Iris’s channel boasts over a thousand subscribers.

5 Iris Caldwell Is Waiting For Mr. Right

Iris Caldwell is keeping the hope that there is a man for her. The North Carolina native is not giving up on finding Mr. Right despite how her first marriage turned out. In one of the former couple’s sessions where they got closure on their relationship, Iris reiterated that she believes there is a man for her.

“Absolutely, there is love out there for me somewhere,” she told the expert. For the model, the show was a learning curve, and she is willing to take the things she learned into a new relationship.

4 Iris Caldwell Is An Influencer

Iris Caldwell is securing the bag through her many influencer deals on Instagram. Following her participation in the show, the MAFS alum grew in popularity and increased her Instagram followership to 206k fans. Iris’s followers are not mere numbers; she enjoys a loyal fanbase that constantly engages her posts.

Due to her decent Instagram followership, the reality TV star has partnered with brands to promote their product. Iris mostly promotes brands along the lines of beauty, hair, and fashion. In 2021, the model bagged a sponsorship deal with alcoholic beverage brand, Hennessy.

3 Iris Caldwell Has Been Traveling

Iris’s Instagram bio gives an insight into her priorities, and traveling is one of them. These days, Iris spends time traveling to choice locations around the world. The Married At First Sight star dedicated a section of her website to her travels. On it, she details her travel experience to new places and shares tips for intending travelers. Since leaving the show, Iris has visited Salt Lake City, Mexico, and St. Lucia.

2 Iris Caldwell Is Firm In Her Faith

Iris Caldwell’s faith is one aspect of her life that has remained unshaken. On the show, Iris was vocal about her religion, and the similarities in faith was the basis of her pairing with Keith Manley. The couples were devout Christians and shared a mutual connection in that light. These days, Iris exercises her belief in Christianity by sharing Bible-inspired messages with her teeming fans. Iris has experienced growth since being on the show, and every time she has attributed her success to God.

1 Iris Caldwell Is Into The Business Of Fashion

Iris Caldwell understands that looking good is good business and has keyed into the fashion industry. In 2021, the reality TV star co-designed The Blooming Pastures Dress with The Adventures Dress brand. Recently, the motivational speaker launched her summer collection in collaboration with Accent Prone. Iris’s inspiration for her dresses stems from her personality and the memorable places she visited. Asides from her co-designing collaborations with brands, the reality TV star promotes apparels for other fashion brands.
