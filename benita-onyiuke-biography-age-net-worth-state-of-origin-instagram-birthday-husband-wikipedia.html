Biography

Benita Onyiuke (born May 29, 1991) is a beautiful Nigerian actress, model, producer, and entrepreneur.

She is the CEO of Bnit Production Movies and Beautyaffairbybnit Salon and won the Scream Awards for best new Actor in 2019.

Benita Onyiuke was born in Enugu State, but she is originally from Enugwu Ukwu in Anambra State.

Early Life

Benita Onyiuke is from a close-knit family of six (6), as revealed by herself in an interview.

Career

Benita Onyiuke started her movie career in 2007, where she was featured in ‘Only daughter’, and after that, she landed her first lead role in the movie ‘Before the war’ where she acted alongside Nonso Diobi, Mercy Johnson other top Nollywood actors.

Benita Onyiuke contested for the most beautiful girl in Nigeria in 2014 and emerged winner, representing Benue State, Nigeria.

She is the CEO of Bnit Production Movies and Beautyaffairbybnit Salon and won the Scream Awards for best new Actor in 2019.

Personal Life

Benita Onyiuke is currently married to her long time boyfriend, Ugochukwu and the union is blessed with three beautiful kids.

Over the years, Benita Onyiuke has attracted herself to prominent movie directors and producers who feature her consistently as a lead character over the years.

She can interpret her role very well and can fit in almost any character given to her. However, she often plays the role of a royal princess, lover girl, village girl, and a maiden.

Benita’s View on ‘Marriage’ in an Interview

Benita Onyiuke, a married mother of three, seems to be enjoying the best of two worlds; a career in Nollywood and marriage.

However, the Anambra State indigene from Enugwu Ukwu agrees there is no such thing as a perfect marriage, even though she wouldn’t trade her marriage for anything in the world.

She said, “: No marriage is perfect. Every marriage has its own challenges, but being rooted in God and being with someone who understands and makes you happy is the real deal. What obtains in a marriage is different from person to person. What may be good for someone may be bad for another.
