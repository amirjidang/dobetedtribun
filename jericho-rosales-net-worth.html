Jericho Rosales is a Filipino singer, songwriter, and actor with a net worth around $3.5 million. He was born in Quezon City in the Philippines where he later proceeded to New Era University for his undergraduate studies. His primary source of income is through acting where he has been featured in various movies. The fact that he speaks fluent English puts him in a good position to act in movies that are not necessarily from the Philippines.

Jericho Rosales’ career started as a model where he got the opportunity to meet with prominent people in the movie industry and consequently got various minor roles. His talent attracted even more movie makers who gave more prominent roles, hence, allowing him to get the fame of a big movie star. In 2001, he also ventured into music where he produced a number of songs. His popularity as a movie star played a significant role in helping his music and making the fans relate with that music more. However, the primary thing that has made him all the money he has is his acting career.
