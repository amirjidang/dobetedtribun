Simon Guobadia Accuses Porsha Williams Of Bringing Armed Man To Home

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Simon Guobadia has made accusations against his estranged wife, Porsha Williams, as they head toward finalizing their divorce.

According to Page Six, Guobadia claims Willimas brought a person carrying a weapon to their shared home on more than one occasion. The court filing detailed that an “armed gunman” accompanied the reality star after she “abandoned” their residence.

When the 42-year-old returned to the home, she allegedly brought along “a man visibly bearing a gun for reasons unknown to [Guobadia].”

Guobadia claims that in one occurrence earlier this month, he was forced to call law enforcement to “maintain the peace.” According to his account Williams, her mother, and her mother’s boyfriend made a “forcible entry” into the home and “tampered with items and evidence.”

The court filing, which requests a judge issue a restraining order against the Real Housewives of Atlanta star claims “Since the filing of the instant divorce, Wife’s actions have been erratic, unstable, threatening, and harassing to … the house staff, and the minor children.”

Williams announced her divorce from Guobadia in February after 15 months of marriage. Amid the split, allegations of Guobadia partaking in fraud between 1988 and 1991 — including using a false identity on his temporary resident pass-through, and not disclosing his criminal past on immigration documents — surfaced. However, they are reportedly not factors in the relationship ending.

The two were engaged in May 2021 after dating for a month and shared two weddings, a traditional Nigerian affair and an American ceremony in November 2022. Williams recently announced her return to television, reviving her main-character status on RHOA, as well as other potential endeavors. The conflict will likely be documented in the drama-filled series.
