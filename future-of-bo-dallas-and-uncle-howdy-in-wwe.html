Backstage Update On Future Of Bo Dallas And Uncle Howdy In WWE

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 5, 2024

Highlights

  • It seems unlikely that Bo Dallas will return to the WWE ring anytime soon, as wrestling insiders do not expect him to be back after his absence following Bray Wyatt's death.
  • Rumors suggest that WWE might bring back the Uncle Howdy character and Firefly Funhouse as a tribute to Bray Wyatt, with WWE Executive Triple H open to creative ideas from the writers.
  • Fans have been speculating that Bo Dallas could continue the Fiend character as a tribute to his brother, and many have expressed support for this concept on social media. Bo Dallas's involvement in a potential WrestleMania 39 storyline with Bray Wyatt is uncertain.

With the passing of Bray Wyatt, it hasn't been clear what the future holds for people who were working with him. in WWE. Specifically, for performers, like his brother Bo Dallas, what was going to happen with his Uncle Howdy character and if it was coming to an end was not clear. Unfortunately, it sounds like he might be done in WWE.

Speculation abounds regarding the future Dallas following his absence from WWE television since the death of his brother, Bray Wyatt. According to wrestling insiders, it appears unlikely that Bo Dallas will be making a return to the WWE ring anytime soon. According to Dave Meltzer of The Wrestling Observer Newsletter, “I think he’s done. The only reason he was even being used was as a favor to Bray Wyatt. It’s possible they’ll use him, but I don’t expect him to be back.”

However, rumors have circulated suggesting a possible return of the Uncle Howdy character and reports are that WWE is not closed off to the idea of bringing back the character and the Firefly Funhouse as a tribute to Bray. WWE Executive Triple H has been open to creative ideas from the writers, including those related to different talents, hinting at the potential revival of Uncle Howdy. It may not be a full-time gig, but all options are on the table as long as it's tasteful and creatively well-planned out.

Fans have been actively engaged in speculation, with some suggesting that WWE should even continue The Fiend character, originally portrayed by Bray Wyatt, with Bo Dallas taking on the role as a tribute to his brother. Fans have seen videos of how much Bo can sound like Bray and the idea has gained traction on social media platforms, with many expressing their support for the concept.

Bo Dallas Was Set To Be Part Of A Big Storyline

In an intriguing twist, Karrion Kross revealed details about a potential angle involving Bray Wyatt at WrestleMania 39. The storyline, which also included Alexa Bliss and Scarlett Bordeaux, was set to involve Bo Dallas. However, this storyline has been left hanging in uncertainty, leaving fans wondering about the fate of Bo Dallas's character in the WWE Universe. Kross, in the meantime, is putting up videos on social media teasing a new direction for his gimmick.

What could have been would have been really entertaining. The hope is that WWE find something creative for Bo Dallas to be involved in.
