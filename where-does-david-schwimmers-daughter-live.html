Where does David Schwimmers daughter live?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

David Schwimmer’s daughter Cleo Buckman Schwimmer splits her time between his home and her mum’s, Zoë Buckman.

David Schwimmer and Zoë Buckman married in June 2010 and divorced in 2017.

Although the former couple is in New York, David lives in East Village, Manhattan, New York, while Zoë Buckman resides in Brooklyn, New York.

Cleo Buckman Schwimmer is the daughter and only child of David Schwimmer.

She was born on May 8, 2011 in United States to David Schwimmer and Zoë Buckman.

Cleo’s father David Schwimmer is an American actor, director and producer. He is known for Friends (1994–2004), Six Days Seven Nights (1998), Band of Brothers (2001) and Big Nothing (2006). While her mother, Zoë Buckman is an English artist, photographer and writer.

At age 9, Cleo decided to ditch her long brown locks in favor of a shaved head, made possible by the expert shearing skills of her mom, Zoë Buckman.

Cleo Buckman Schwimmer is 12 years old as of 2022.
