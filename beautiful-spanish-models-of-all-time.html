Blanca Padilla

Blanca Padilla is a renowned Spanish fashion model who has taken the fashion industry by storm. Known for her striking beauty and captivating presence on the runway, she has become a prominent figure in the world of fashion.

Blanca rose to fame after her appearance at the Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in Madrid, where she showcased her extraordinary talent and caught the attention of designers and fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

Her remarkable career has seen her walking the runways of renowned fashion cities such as Paris, Milan, and New York. Blanca has become a favorite among top designers, and she has had the privilege of collaborating with fashion powerhouses like Dolce & Gabbana and Valentino.

In 2017, Blanca made history as the first Spanish model to don the iconic Victoria’s Secret Angel wings, solidifying her status as a supermodel and further propelling her career to new heights.

With her exceptional beauty, grace, and talent, Blanca Padilla continues to inspire and captivate audiences around the world. She truly is a force to be reckoned with in the fashion industry.

Aida Domènech

Aida Domènech is not just an ordinary Spanish model; she is a fashion influencer and blogger who has made a significant impact in the fashion industry. Starting her blog in 2009, Aida has garnered a massive following on Instagram, where she showcases her impeccable style and fashion expertise. In 2014, her talent and dedication to fashion earned her the prestigious Best Style Fashion Blog award at Berlin Fashion Week.

With her extensive knowledge and unique sense of style, Aida has become a fashion mentor to many aspiring fashion enthusiasts. She shares her tips, advice, and fashion inspirations through her blog, enabling her followers to embrace their individuality and express themselves through fashion. Aida’s influence extends beyond the digital realm; she has also authored books and established her own perfume and clothing brand, solidifying her presence as a major player in the fashion world.

Clara Alonso

Clara Alonso is a talented Spanish model who has made waves in the fashion industry by collaborating with renowned brands such as Guess and AX Armani Exchange. With her stunning looks and graceful catwalk presence, Clara has left an indelible mark on the world of fashion.

In 2008, Clara achieved a significant milestone in her career by becoming the third Spanish model to walk in the prestigious Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show. This coveted opportunity solidified her status as a top model and showcased her ability to captivate audiences on a global stage.

Not limited to her work on the runway, Clara has also established herself as a prominent fashion blogger for Vogue Spain. Through her blog, she provides a glimpse into her glamorous life and shares her personal style, inspiring fashion enthusiasts around the world.

Clara Alonso’s beauty, talent, and passion for fashion continue to shape the industry, making her an iconic figure in the modeling world.
