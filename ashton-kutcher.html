Ashton Kutcher News - Us Weekly

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Name:Ashton Kutcher

Hometown:Cedar Rapids, IA

Birthday:February 7, 1978

About

After hitting it big with the late ‘90s Fox sitcom That ‘70s Show, Ashton Kutcher‘s sophomoric frat-house humor, trendy shaggy hairstyle and connections with the MTV Punk’d crowd landed him in a number of hit films.

Born in 1978 in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, Kutcher — who has a twin brother — has taken on a number of genres throughout his career, starring in films from Dude, Where’s My Car? to more serious flicks including The Butterfly Effect and The Guardian.

Outside of his acting career, Kutcher is a major tech investor and cofounder of the firms A-Grade Investments and Sound Ventures. He was an early investor in Uber and Airbnb.

Kutcher said “I do” to Demi Moore in a Jewish Kabbalistic ceremony in 2005. Seven years later, he filed to divorce the actress in December 2012. After reconnecting with his former That ‘70s Show costar Mila Kunis, he wed the actress in July 2015. The pair share two children, daughter Wyatt Isabelle Kutcher (born October 2014) and son Dimitri Portwood Kutcher (born November 2016).
