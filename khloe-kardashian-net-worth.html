Khloe Kardashian is an American reality TV star, producer, and businesswoman, and has an estimated net worth of $40 million. Born Khloe Alexandra Kardashian on June 27, 1984, she is the daughter of Kris Jenner and late attorney Robert Kardashian. Khloe is the younger sister of Kim and Kourtney Kardashian.

She shot to fame in the mid-to-late-2000s after appearing on the E! network's reality show, Keeping Up With the Kardashians. The show followed the lives of the family on a regular basis, with the focus on her older sister Kim and her efforts to boost her rocky public profile. With her sisters Kourtney and Kim, she designed a clothing line, Kardashian Kollection for Sears, opened a new location of the family boutique, DASH, in Manhattan's Soho, and celebrated the launch of Kardashian Khaos, a specialty store in Las Vegas' Mirage hotel. She also hosted a radio show for local radio station 102.9.
