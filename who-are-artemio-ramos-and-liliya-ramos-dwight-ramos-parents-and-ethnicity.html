Who Are Artemio Ramos And Liliya Ramos? Dwight Ramos Parents And Ethnicity

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Artemio Ramos and Liliya Ramos are the parents of Dwight Ramos. While the Levanga Hokkaido Pointguard’s dad is from Ilocos Sur, his mom is from Russia.

Dwight Ramos is a professional basketball player for the Japanese B League team, Levanga Hokkaido. The point guard graduated from the University of the Athletic Association of the Philippines (UAAP).

The athlete’s fans worldwide are eager to learn more about his personal life, including his family members. Check out the details about Dwight Ramos’s parents below.

Meet Dwight Ramos Parents: Artemio Ramos And Liliya Ramos

Artemio Ramos and Liliya Ramos are the parents of Dwight Ramos. They welcomed the talented basketball professional on 2 September 1998 as their first child.

While the young athlete’s dad is from Ilocos Sur, his mom is from Russia. Thus, he has mixed ethnicity.

His younger sibling, Eli Ramos, was born in 2000 and is also into basketball. Earlier this year, Dwight Ramos’ brother left the Ateneo Blue Eagles and joined Adamson Falcons. Emma Ramos is Dwight Ramos’ sister, who is still young.

The Filipino-American athlete regularly updates his professional life to his fans and follower. There are only a few pictures together with his family.

While the basketball player’s dad is not present on Instagram, his mom’s account is private. Regardless, the Ramos family is undoubtedly supportive and caring of one another.

Who Is Dwight Ramos Girlfriend?

The Levanga Hokkaido official has been in a loving relationship for a few years. Kim Kianna Dy is Dwight Ramos’ girlfriend.

The adorable pair appears to have been in love for a few years. In addition, they are head over heels for one another. The smitten couple doesn’t shy away from expressing their love and affection, and often shares snap of themselves enjoying one another’s Company.

Dwight Ramos’ girlfriend, Kim, is a Filipino professional volleyball player. She plays for the Philippines women’s national volleyball team and the PVL team Logistics Cargo Movers.

The professional volleyball player was born on 26 July 1995. That made her three-year older than her boyfriend.

As both of them are involved in sports, the adorable pair must have crossed paths over their work circle. Despite their demanding career, the lovebirds’ union is going strong. We wish the athlete-pair a lasting bond.

Dwight Ramos Career Explored

The Filipino-American basketball player completed his formal education at Walnut High School. During his days at the high school, he played under the head coach Joe khouzam.

The Ateneo de Manila University contacted Dwight to play for their team Blue Eagles. While Ateneo was a good option, the talented player decided not to continue his college basketball career in the Philippines.

He enrolled at California State University and continued his playing career with Cal State Fullerton Titans. During his time in the NCAA Division I from 2016 to 2018, he played for the Broncos of California State Polytechnic University in Pomona for one season.

Afterward, Ramos went to the Phillippines to play for the Ateneo, but before he could suit up, he had to serve a residency in 2019. He was more than ready to make his debut in the UAAP season 83.

But, the season was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. For the chance to play alongside his brother in Ateneo, he opted not to enter the PBA draft in 2021.

At that time, he joined Toyama Grouses of B League. Ramos remained in the league, and the rest is history.
