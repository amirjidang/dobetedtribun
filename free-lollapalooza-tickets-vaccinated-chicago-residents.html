Chicago Giving Free Lollapalooza Tickets To Vaccinated Residents

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 10, 2024

The entire world is part of a full throttle mission to vaccinate as much of the population, as quickly as possible, to curb the spread of COVID-19. In an interesting new twist, the residents of Chicago now have another reason to get their COVID vaccine. The city of Chicago is offering a very appealing, incredibly enticing offer to their local community, and it comes in the form of free Lollapalooza tickets! Mass Live reports that those who were already thinking of getting their tickets to the show, can now get them for absolutely free when they go to get fully vaccinated. Others people who may have been vaccine hesitant, now have new motivation to take the next steps. The incentive is huge, as the cost of each one day ticket is regularly priced at $225.

As various regions struggle to vaccinate the population and protect their communities against COVID-19, Chicago has come up with a brilliant idea that has many people not just willing, but actually eager and ready to get their shots. Giving away Lollapalooza tickets as a reward to those who agree to get fully vaccinated against the Coronavirus is proving to be a successful method to quickly vaccinate many more citizens.

Residents of Chicago are now able to book their vaccinations and receive the ultimate incentive - a free day pass to Lollapalooza.

The incredibly organized process sees various locations offering tickets to different dates, so that citizens can properly coordinate their schedules and attend the right vaccination clinic. Those looking to obtain tickets for the July 29th date can attend Wilbur Wright College to get vaccinated. Richard J. Daley College will give away passes for July 30, Kennedy King College has July 31 passes and Malcolm X College will offer August 1 tickets.

According to MXDWN, this year's Lollapallooza festival will "feature artists such as Foo Fighters, Tyler, the Creator, Post Malone, Miley Cyrus, Rico Nasty, Megan Thee Stallion, slowthai, BROCKHAMPTON, DaBaby, Playboi Carti, EarthGang, Young Thug, Band of Horses, Modest Mouse, Limp Bizkit and many more."

There will also be tickets available to those who are already fully vaccinated, and those general ticket sales become available for purchase on June the 27th.
