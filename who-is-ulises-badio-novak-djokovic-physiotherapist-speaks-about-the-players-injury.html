Ulises Badio Wikipedia: Who Is Novak Djokovic Physiotherapist?

Novak Djokovic’s physiotherapist, Ulises Badio, is an Argentine doctor who specializes in kinesiology, physiotherapy, acupuncture, and Chinese medicine.

Although he doesn’t have a dedicated Wikipedia bio to this date, Badio’s work resume and incredible assistance to the athletes down the years speak for his brilliance. The doctor joined Djokovic in 2017 after serving several years as the physiotherapist at Valencia CF, one of the best football clubs in Spain.

He has been with the athlete for around five years, and the Serbian player has never shied from speaking fondly of his helper. Novak has previously praised Ulises’s professionalism, excellency, humanity, and how he exactly knows his work and excels at them.
