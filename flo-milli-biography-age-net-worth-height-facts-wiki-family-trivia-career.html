Flo Milli is an American rapper who is well recognized for her raw and honest vocals in her popular singles, “Beef FloMix”, “In The Party” and “Not Friendly”. The songs went viral on several social media platforms, especially on TikTok. Having her career started in 2015, Milli has managed to come a long way up becoming one of the popular pop singers of 2020. On July 24, 2020, she released her debut mixtape, “Ho, Why Is You Here?”. 

With her up growing popularity, she has managed to amass a fan following over 896k followers on Instagram: @flomillishit and over 357k followers on Twitter: @_FloMilli.

When was Flo Milli Born?

Flo Milli was born on January 9, 2000, in Mobile, Alabama, United States. Her birth name is Tamia Monique Carter. Her nationality is American. Carter belongs to the African-American ethnicity while Capricon is her zodiac sign. 

Born and raised in her hometown, Mobile, Alabama, Flo Milli attended Collier Elementary where she starred as Winkie in the play, “Peter Pan”. Growing up, she listened to  Jill Scott, Anthony Hamilton, and Erykah Badu, shortly after developed an interest in rapping that she even wrote her first song at 9 years old, and began rapping at age 11. 

She even formed a rap group “Real & Beautiful”, later known as Pink Mafia, which however dissolved when she was 14. She was inspired by the show “106 & Park” that featured Nicki Minaj, that she got inspired and began writing short verses that grew into songs. 

Flo Milli Rapper Career

  • Flo Milli started her career releasing her breakout single, “Beef FloMix” in 2018. 
  • The song went viral on social media sites on TikTok and Instagram that the song reached no. 2 on Spotify’s Viral 50 in April 2019. 
  • Her next single was “In The Party” which was released the same year. 
  • She even got a chance to perform at Rolling Loud Los Angeles in December 2019. 
  • Just within a year in the industry, she got signed to RCA Records and ’94 Sounds by the end of 2019. 
  • She released the single, “My Attitude” in 2020. 
  • In March and April 2020, her singles, “Not Friendly” and “Eat It Up” were released. 
  • She released the J. White Did It-produced single “Like That Bitch” in June 2020.
  • Her debut mixtape titled “Ho, Why Is You Here?”, was released on July 24, 2020. 
  • She then signed an exclusive global co-publishing deal with Pulse Music Group in August 2020. 
  • She was also featured in the music video for G-Eazy’s single, “Down”, in September 2020. 
  • Her single titled “Roaring 20s” in January 2021. 
  • Her latest rap single, “Ice Baby” was released in October 2021. 
  • Besides, she also has her self-titled YouTube channel with over 150k subscribers.
