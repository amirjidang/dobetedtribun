We need every kind of sandal to feel fulfilled in the summer. A flat flip-flop, a platform, a strappy gladiator, a slide and, of course, a heel sandal! All of that open-toe goodness combined with the elongating class of a heel? Heaven. But only if it’s comfortable and easy to walk in!

The Madden Girl Bella Two-Piece Block Heel Sandals are footwear goals. Not just for Us, but for everyone, hence why these shoes already have over 300 reviews! These sandals were already under $50 to start with, but with Macy’s latest sale, they’re officially less than $35 now — but only for a limited time!

See it: Get the Madden Girl Bella Two-Piece Block Heel Sandals (originally $49) for just $30 (40% off) at Macy’s! Offer ends July 7, 2019.

So what are all of these reviewers saying? That these are “the most comfortable heels ever,” for starters. One said they’re actually comfortable enough “to wear all day” without any foot pain or blisters, even from the first wear! Everyone obviously loves the look, calling them “so gorgeous” that they bought multiple pairs. One shopper said they’re great to “wear with jeans for a casual look” as well as “with a sundress,” or even out at night. Another assured that they will make us the “standout” guest anywhere we go!

These sandals have a 3 ½-inch chunky block heel, so they provide a substantial amount of height, but are sturdy and easy to walk in. On some versions, the heel is wrapped to match the upper, but not all! While this heel is chunky, the strap up at the ankle is actually thin and delicate, but strong enough to hold us in place. It’s adjustable, too!

These sandals have a round toe with one strap running across our foot. As for materials, the upper varies depending on which version of this Bella shoe we choose. Some are soft, some are smooth and some are textured in unexpected ways! There are seven versions currently available, so let’s check them out!

See it: Get the Madden Girl Bella Two-Piece Block Heel Sandals (originally $49) for just $30 (40% off) at Macy’s! Offer ends July 7, 2019.

As for soft, solid-colored versions, we have Bright Red, Black and Yellow, which is a mustard shade. For something smoother, we should look at the Blush Smooth version, a pretty nude that will go with just about anything. For a slightly lighter shade, there’s Natural Multi, which is also smooth, with the exception of the toe strap. This strap is woven with multiple pastel shades! It’s so pretty, and we love the exposed, stacked heel!

Other fun versions include Black Multi, which has a black and white chevron pattern woven onto the toe strap, and Bright Snake Multi. This shade is, of course, snake print, but it’s no neutral boa constrictor pattern. It has fun splashes of yellow, blue, red and white!

Madden Girl, a sister brand of Steve Madden, is “the embodiment of individuality with just a bit of edge.” Our individuality and personality are on full display in these shoes with all of the different versions to choose from! We’re feeling a bit sassy today, so the snake-print is the way to go, but who knows what tomorrow will bring?
