Stage Career

In addition to her work on screen and in the studio, Sabrina Carpenter has also pursued a successful career on stage. Her versatility as an artist extends to the theatrical realm, where she has showcased her talent and captivated audiences.

In 2015, Carpenter took on the role of Wendy in the Pasadena Playhouse production of “Peter Pan and Tinker Bell: A Pirate’s Christmas.” This enchanting performance allowed her to delve into the magical world of Peter Pan and bring the beloved character to life on stage.

Continuing to make waves in the theater industry, Carpenter made her highly anticipated Broadway debut in the production of “Mean Girls” as Cady Heron in 2020. Unfortunately, the run was cut short due to the COVID-19 pandemic, but Carpenter’s talent and dedication were evident in her portrayal of the iconic character.

Through her stage career, Sabrina Carpenter has proven her ability to transition seamlessly between different mediums of performance.
