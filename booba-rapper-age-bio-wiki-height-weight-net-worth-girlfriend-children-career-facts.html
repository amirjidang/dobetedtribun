Booba (Rapper) Age, Bio, Wiki, Height, Weight, Net Worth, Girlfriend, Children, Career, Facts

Elie Yaffa is better known by his stage name ‘Booba’. He is a French Rapper from Boulogne-Billancourt, France. He is one of the highest paid rapper in the world. In 1990s, he began his career as a break dancer and rose his fame by forming the band “Lunatic”, in 1994 with his close friend named ‘Ali’. Later, both of them released a critically acclaimed album. Then, in 2003 they got separated. After this,the beautiful and adventurous journey of Elie began alone. In his career till date, he sold over 10 million copies of his albums. He is one the well known and successful musician in the industry. He also established his own rap label named “Tallac Records”. He also scored his third platinum-selling album with “Futur”. In fact, he also own his merchandise named, ‘Booba’ where he sells hoodies, T-shirts and different products of his own print. Other than all of these, he has a huge fan following on his social media platforms. He has over 4.5 million+ followers on his instagram account under user name ‘boobaofficial‘. He runs his own youtube channel named ‘B20baOfficiel‘ where he has over 3 million+ subscribers as of Dec, 2019. His snapchat user id is ‘bilton-92’. He is also on TikTok under user name ‘bilton92’.
