Peter Cullen Biography

Peter Claver Cullen is a voice actor from Canada. He is best known for providing the voice of Optimus Prime in the original Transformers animated series from the 1980s, as well as most other incarnations of the character.

In addition to Eeyore in the Winnie the Pooh franchise, Monterey Jack in Chip ‘n Dale Rescue Rangers, the first voice of KARR in Knight Rider, and the vocalizations of the title character in Predator, he has voiced many other characters in a variety of popular media. Beginning with the first live-action film in 2007, Cullen reprised his role as Optimus Prime in Transformers media.
