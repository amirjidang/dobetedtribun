The internet age is upon us. It was inevitable since a decade or so ago in its infancy, it was just a source of research material, a luxury for most. Now it has become more than that. A lot more. Not only has it been a major source of entertainment, the use of the internet has evolved into a way of life, almost a necessity even.

We wake up and the first thing we do is check our phone. We check our mail, check our news feed. The use of the internet has intertwined with our lives. As with any source of entertainment, stars will be born.

Enter Lele Pons, internet personality and one of the most famous, if not most famous person in the now defunct video platform Vine. Despite being known primarily as a quirky comedian, the Venezuelan persona is also known for her goddess like beauty and great fashion sense.

Considering the nature of her videos, it’s no surprise that Lele Pons is a free spirited lass. Being quirky, one could think that Lele Pons is the type of girl who doesn’t care about her looks. Far from it, she’s a lady through and through.

Boyish attitude aside, Lele is known to sport curvy cocktail designer dresses and comfortable everyday clothes with such charm we can’t stop looking at her.

Her comfort in her own skin allows her to wear outfits with confidence. Lele Pons is known to rock any outfit and give it a style of her own.

Lele sure knows how to wear a monochromatic attire while keeping her and our lives colorful!

Designer Denim Diva

Fun fact: Jeans are pants made from denim or dungee cloth and was believed to be first made for miners during the Gold Rush era by Levi Strauss and his tailor friend Jacob Davis. It wasn’t until the 1930’s that women were able to wear and enjoy a pair of pants.

Through the years, the use of denim has expanded and evolved that it’s no longer used just for making pants but it has also been used to make other types of garments as well such as jackets, skirts and shorts among other things. This is because denim has an undeniably classic and rugged look.

As seen in the picture, Lele Pons is captured wearing a denim long coat paired with thigh high denim peep toe high heeled boots. Denim clothes are no longer confined in the notion of being a workman’s clothes. Style icons like Lele Pons, is a clear testament that denim clothes can be fun and functional at the same time, all the time.
