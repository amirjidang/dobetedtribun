Here Are How Much Lady Gaga's Ex-Boyfriend's Are Really Worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Ever since Lady Gaga first rose to fame, it has been clear that she is an extremely talented performer. In fact, as a result of Gaga’s impressive vocal skills, her songwriting abilities, and the fact that she works with the best producers, she is one of the few singers of her generation who has staying power. On top of that, she has proven to be a talented actor who was so believable in A Star Is Born that people believed something went on between Gaga and Bradley Cooper.

Since Lady Gaga has managed to have a huge impact on pop culture, her fans and the media have been very interested in her private life. For example, whenever she starts dating someone, everyone wants to know what they can about the people Gaga has dated. On top of that, there are a lot of rumors about people Gaga may have dated even though the relationships are unconfirmed. Given the curiosity about the people Gaga has dated or been connected to through rumors, it is interesting to compare them by looking at their reported net worths.

6 Tara Savelo Is Worth $1.4 Million

Throughout Lady Gaga’s time in the public eye, she has garnered a lot of attention for the amazing outfits that she has worn in public. Even though it is Gaga’s clothes that get the lion’s share of the attention, she also has her hair and makeup on point whenever she appears in public. With that in mind, it shouldn’t surprise anyone that Gaga employs a hair and makeup team. On the other hand, some Gaga fans won’t know that in 2009 it was rumored that the singer was involved with a makeup artist she employed named Tara Savelo. According to networthpost.org, Savelo’s current net worth is $1.4 million but reliable sources have yet to confirm that figure.

5 Dan Horton Is Worth $1.9 Million

Evidently, Lady Gaga has a thing for dating the people who work for or with her. After all, on top of the fact that there were rumors that Gaga dated her aforementioned makeup artist, it has been confirmed that she was involved with Dan Horton in 2019. According to his LinkedIn page, Horton began working as a monitor engineer for Gaga in 2018. On top of serving in that role for Gaga and other artists like Bruno Mars, Camilla Cabello, and Justin Timberlake, Horton also is the owner of Audio Engineering Consulting Group. According to networthpost.org, Horton’s current net worth is $1.9 million but reliable sources have yet to confirm that figure.

4 Christian Carino Is Worth $5 Million

From the outside looking in, the entertainment industry seems like it should be simple. If an actor or musician is talented, they should get the opportunities. In reality, however, a lot of other things go into whether or not musicians and actors succeed, including how good their representatives are. Before Christian Carino dated Lady Gaga from 2017 until 2019, he was hired as an agent for CAA, the company that represents people like Beyoncé, Daft Punk, Harry Styles, Justin Bieber, Lana Del Rey. Due to the role that people like him wield in the business, Carino is worth $5 million according to nickiswift.com.

3 Taylor Kinney Is Worth $8 Million

As of the time of this writing, Taylor Kinney is far from a household name. However, Kinney has enjoyed a lot of success as an actor due to starring in the show The Vampire Diaries, as well as movies like Zero Dark Thirty and The Other Woman. However, Kinney’s biggest career achievement to date is playing Chicago Fire’s Lieutenant Kelly Severide since 2012. As a result of all those roles and others that weren’t listed here, Kinney has amassed an $8 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

2 Angelina Jolie’s Net Worth Is $120 Million

Not too long after she became famous, a rumor that Lady Gaga and Angelina Jolie were having an affair surfaced in 2010. At that time and in the years since, neither Gaga nor Jolie have confirmed or denied the rumor so there is no way to know if there ever was any truth to it at all. On the other hand, it is abundantly clear that Jolie is one of the biggest movie stars of her generation. For that reason, Jolie has been able to demand the kinds of paycheques that have allowed her to build a $120 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

1 Michael Polansky Is Worth $600 Million

As of the time of this writing, it is believed that Lady Gaga and Michael Polansky are still a couple after starting their relationship in 2020. Given the fact that Gaga called Polansky “the man I love” and “my whole life” during a 2021 Hollywood Reporter interview, it seems like he has made her very happy which is wonderful. On top of being Gaga’s boyfriend, Polansky also is a Harvard grad and tech entrepreneur who has been the CEO of Facebook co-founder Sean Parker’s business, The Parker Group for more than a decade. Thanks to the massive salary he makes from that role, Polansky is worth $600 million according to The Sun.
