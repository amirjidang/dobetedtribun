St. Louis Blues Logo History

The history of the St. Louis Blues logo stretches back to the team’s inception in 1967. The original logo featured a blue and yellow shield adorned with crossed swords and stars, along with the letters “StL” in white. As the team evolved, so did the logo, with various modifications made over the years to represent the team’s identity and history.

In subsequent iterations, the St. Louis Blues logo underwent changes that included the addition of the word “Blues” and the introduction of the iconic Blue Note symbol. The Blue Note, a musical symbol, became a central element in the team’s logo, signifying the rich musical heritage of St. Louis. This distinctive symbol, outlined in yellow, white, and blue, became a beloved emblem of the franchise.

In 2009, the St. Louis Blues unveiled their current logo, which is a refined version of previous designs. The focus of the updated logo was simplicity, while still maintaining the iconic Blue Note symbol. With minor adjustments and different shades of blue, the current logo captures the pride and history of the St. Louis Blues.

The St. Louis Blues logo’s evolution has been a reflection of the team’s journey, from its beginnings in 1967 to becoming a beloved franchise in the NHL. The logo represents the team’s identity and the connection it shares with the passionate fans in St. Louis.

Evolution of the St. Louis Blues Logo

Since its inception in 1967, the St. Louis Blues logo has evolved and transformed, representing the team’s history and identity. Let’s explore the significant changes that have shaped the St. Louis Blues logo over the years.

The original logo, introduced in 1967, featured a blue and yellow shield with crossed swords and stars, symbolizing strength and courage. This design represented the team’s determination to conquer the challenges they faced on the ice.

In 1998, the St. Louis Blues logo underwent a significant update, as the team embraced a modernized look. The curved lines and vibrant colors added a dynamic and contemporary vibe to the logo. The most notable change was the introduction of a larger letter ‘B’ inside an oval shape, giving the logo a sleek and bold appearance.

The current St. Louis Blues logo, unveiled in 2009, is a refined version of its predecessors. It maintains the essential elements of the previous designs but incorporates subtle changes to enhance its visual impact. The logo retains the iconic Blue Note symbol, wings, and color palette, representing the team’s rich history and connection to the city of St. Louis.
