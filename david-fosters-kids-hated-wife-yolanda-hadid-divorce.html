David Foster's Kids Loathed Yolanda Hadid Before And After Their Divorce

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 23, 2024

Highlights

  • David Foster's children, Sara and Erin Foster, have publicly expressed their dislike of Yolanda Hadid.
  • Sara and Erin Foster are not supportive of Yolanda's focus on her Lyme Disease illness.
  • Yolanda and David's divorce was not solely caused by her Lyme disease.

Thanks to his lengthy career composing music and her spot on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, David Foster and Yolanda Hadid were quite the power couple when they were married. After tying the knot in 2011, they got divorced in 2017, and fans of RHOBH were stunned to hear that the seemingly happy pair were splitting up. Yolanda doted on David in every scene, and they appeared to be totally in love.

David Foster, now married to his fifth wife, has six children, and some of them have been outspoken about his time with the former Real Housewife. Here's why David Foster's children aren't big fans of Yolanda Hadid.

David Foster's Kids Have Been Critical Of His Ex-wife Yolanda Hadid

According to Nicki Swift, Sara and Erin Foster don't like Yolanda Hadid. The publication noted that although they didn't comment on their father David Foster's marriage, once they knew that David and Yolanda were splitting up, they began sharing their opinions. Sara and Erin think that Yolanda's approach to Lyme disease is odd, and this seems to be the main problem that they have.

E! News reported that Sara and Erin Foster appeared on the podcast Dirty, Sexy, Funny hosted by Jenny McCarthy and discussed how they feel about Yolanda Hadid.

Erin said that when people suffer from an illness, they often hide how bad they feel and they want their battle to be private. She said, "No one wants everyone to know how sick they are and everyone to see how much they're struggling, and when that seems to be the focus, making everyone see how sick you are, that's just confusing to someone who's trying to be supportive."

According to E! News, Sara said of Yolanda:

"Look, this is how I feel. We don't comment to our friends on Yolanda's health. That's not for us to comment on. Only she knows her journey, but what I will say is if you're capable of continuing to be on a reality show, you're not dying. Let's be honest."

It's never easy when someone's parents get divorced, and seeing them with a new spouse can be difficult and painful. However, Sara and Erin Foster have otherwise been supportive of their father's dating life, and they genuinely want him to be in a good partnership.

After news broke of Yolanda Hadid and David Foster's divorce, Sara and Erin Foster commented on their dad's love life and said they thought he would have another wife in the future. According to Page SIX, Erin explained to Ryan Seacrest, "We just want him to be happy. We love when he is married, in a relationship. He is at his happiest when he is in a relationship. He will definitely get married again. He loves being married. Lucky No. 5!”

In 2012, Yolanda Hadid found out she had Lyme disease, according to People. In 2021, she told the publication that she was "better than I've been in a long time." However, in 2022, Us Weekly reported that Yolanda talked about having a "relapse."

David Foster's children Sara and Erin Foster aren't the only people who wondered what the real story was. Yolanda Hadid's Lyme disease was the focus of many RHOBH storylines as she sometimes seemed to be sick. Other times, she seemed to be having fun and like she was full of energy.

What Was The Reason For Yolanda Hadid And David Foster's Divorce?

When Erin and Sara Foster spoke about their dislike of Yolanda Hadid, they made it clear that they thought she acted strangely about her Lyme disease. It sounds like Yolanda's illness could be why she and David went their separate ways.

In 2015, ABC News reported that Yolanda was interviewed on Andy Cohen's Watch What Happens Live and delved into her upcoming divorce. She answered a question about whether the marriage was ending because of her Lyme disease.

Yolanda said that things became complicated between them and explained, "Well, chronic long-term illness is extremely challenging. Not only for the person sick but also for the caregiver. It for sure changed the dynamic of the relationship." She also said the relationship "was not strong enough to withstand the circumstances that came our way."

However, Yolanda's ex-husband said he didn't decide that the marriage was over because of her disease.

According to Us Weekly, David Foster talked about the divorce in his documentary David Foster: Off The Record and said:

“How can I leave a sick woman? The fact of the matter is, that is not the reason why I left. It was for a different reason, which I will never disclose, but it had nothing to do with her being sick."
