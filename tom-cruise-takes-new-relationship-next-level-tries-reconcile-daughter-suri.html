Tom Cruise is apparently taking his relationship with Elsina Khayrova to the next level. According to various reports, the actor has made their relationship official, which could signal they’ll make their red carpet debut sometime soon.

“It’s well known within Elsina’s circle that she and Tom are now an item,” a source told Daily Mail. “They’ve grown very close over the past few weeks but take a lot of care not to be photographed together because they want to maintain their privacy.”

Tom Is Official With His New Girlfriend Elsina Khayrova

The couple were first romantically linked in December 2023 when they attended a party in London together and reportedly weren’t afraid for other guests to infer their relationship status. Still, the pair were apparently careful not to pose for any images they may surface online, as they’re trying to keep their high-profile relationship on the down low.

The same source emphasized how well things are going between the pair, despite their nearly 30-year age gap – Tom is 61-years old, while Elsina is 36.

“They enjoy hanging out together and despite their wealth, do a lot of the things that normal couples do. They were inseparable, clearly a couple. He seemed to be besotted with her,” the source revealed.

Elsina is one of few women Tom has been connected to romantically in recent years. The Mission: Impossible star has become notorious for keeping his persona life to himself since his infamous divorce from Katie Holmes, which rumors suggested was largely due to his Scientology beliefs. Their divorce was finalized within weeks, without Tom challenging Katie’s request for primary custody of their daughter Suri, now 17, though Tom has been paying a sizeable sum in child support ever since.

Tom Reportedly Wants To Reconcile With Suri When She Starts College

Suri is set to start college later this year, with reports claiming the teen is narrowing down which option. She apparently wants to remain in New York where she’s been living with Katie since her divorce from Tom in 2012. However, it’s been reported that Tom has agreed to continue financially supporting his daughter even when she’s no longer a minor.

Even more, there’s been speculation that Tom is hoping to reconcile with Suri. The actor allegedly hasn’t seen his daughter in years but has hope that’ll change once she becomes a legal adult. Who knows, perhaps it’ll also be a chance to introduce Suri to Elsina, should their relationship continue to go as smoothly as reports claim it is.
