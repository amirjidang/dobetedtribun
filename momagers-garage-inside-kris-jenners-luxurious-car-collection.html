Still, the company treated Justin Bieber similarly after making unauthorized changes to his model. Ferrari asserts that it maintains the right to determine whether to produce unique versions, Spanish daily Marca said.

Additionally, for the first 18 months following purchase, Ferrari prohibits the sale of specified edition vehicles once more. Thankfully, Kris and the Kardashian-Jenner clan already have an adequate number of cars. Take a look at the spectacular collection of the family matriarch.

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Ghost is owned by the star of Keeping Up with the Kardashians, according to O'Gara Coach. Another preferred element of the US$332,500 four-wheel drive is the rear-wheel steering. The matriarch of the Kardashian family also had a white Dawn convertible from the same brand and a black Cullinan convertible worth US$335,000.
