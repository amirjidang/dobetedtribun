Teen Mom Star David Eason Flaunts New Girlfriend As Divorce With Jenelle Evans Continues

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Highlights

  • David Eason's divorce from Jenelle Evans continues, with a postponed court hearing.
  • Jenelle moves on with August Keen, while David starts a new relationship with Kenleigh.
  • David's past legal issues and public persona impact his new relationship with Kenleigh.

David Eason has apparently already moved on from Teen Mom alum Jenelle Evans, as he took to social media to showcase his new girlfriend even though their divorce wages on.

David And Jenelle’s Divorce Isn’t Going As Planned

According to an exclusive report from The Ashley, the former couple was set to meet in court on Monday to tackle several important issues related to their ongoing divorce. While Jenelle had legally separated from David in February, she is not eligible to officially file for divorce until they have been apart for a full year.

During the scheduled court session, the pair was expected to discuss a range of topics including the custody arrangements for their daughter, Ensley, the terms of child support, and how to divide their shared assets. This division of property likely includes deciding who will retain ownership of their property and other shared assets, including their boat.

However, the hearing did not proceed as planned. At the judge’s request, the case has been postponed and will now be revisited later this summer.

David’s New Girlfriend Looks Like Jenelle

Meanwhile, Jenelle has been busy moving on. She has been romantically involved with August Keen, her manager as well as new romantic partner. Jenelle has openly talked about August on her show, Teen Mom: Next Chapter, and has even relocated to Las Vegas to be near him, suggesting a serious commitment to this new relationship.

On the other hand, David has also been making headlines with his new love interest, a woman named Kenleigh. David, who has reportedly been living on a couple’s dilapidated boat, has shared several videos on social media showcasing his time at Kenleigh’s house.

Kenleigh, who is 25 years old (a full decade younger than David)and works in photography and graphic design, has been seen on social media flaunting her home in North Carolina.

Although Jenelle has not made any public statements about David's new relationship, many fans and observers have noted that Kenleigh bears a striking resemblance to Jenelle, sparking discussions among followers about the similarities between the two women.

As both David and Jenelle move forward with their separate lives, it’s clear that their personal dramas continue to captivate the public's attention.

David Eason's past is marred by a series of legal issues. Before his relationship with Jenelle, he had faced multiple arrests for offenses including drug possession and assault. His criminal history is a significant aspect of his public persona. David's controversial actions included a 2018 incident where he killed the family dog, Nugget, which led to a temporary loss of Jenelle’s children and widespread condemnation.

Despite this, David and Kenleigh's relationship appears to be a fresh start for him, offering a glimpse into his post-Jenelle life. Fans may even get to hear Jenelle’s perspective on things now that she’s returned to the Teen Mom franchise.
