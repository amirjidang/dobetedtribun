Mila Kunis Looks Ready to Pop as She Goes for Mani/Pedi: Pic

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

She's blooming! Mila Kunis was literally busting out of her maternity top as she popped in for a last-minute pamper session at a Sherman Oaks, Calif., nail salon on Wednesday, Sept. 10.

The mom-to-be, who is expecting her first daughter with fiancé Ashton Kutcher within the next few weeks, made time for some pre-birth beauty treatments before her bundle of joy makes an appearance.

And judging by the size of her bump, that could be soon! Wearing a slouchy pair of blue sweatpants and a black tank top, Kunis, 31, was unable to cover the entirety of her ever-growing belly. See more pics of Mila's baby bump.
