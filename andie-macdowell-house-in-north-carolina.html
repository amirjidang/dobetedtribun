Andie MacDowell House In North Carolina

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, August 26, 2024

Andie MacDowell’s North Carolina Home – Estimated Value - $3 Million

The famous model and actress Andie MacDowell has a Tudor styled home in the prestigious community of Biltmore forest, North Carolina. This is kind of a dreamy fairytale home. Here are some of its pictures. Do take a look at them.

This lovely cottage home is a great example of meticulous craftsmanship and excellent artistic details. The cozy interiors come with charming detailing all over. Its picturesque appearance will remind you of the beauty of lovely country homes.

Andie’s home has six bedrooms, a study room, family room, beautiful old fireplaces, and there are seven bathrooms. There are beamed ceilings, and a lot of antiques things that give it the Old English and rustic feel. This is a home that blends modernity with the old-world charm wonderfully well. You will find modern equipment such as the Crestron wireless touch-screen controllers and Lutron lighting control. The fower and conservatory are among the other notable features.

Its entryway opens to the cozy interiors. The Great room looks quite dreamy with a small fireplace. The Gathering room is highlighted with a brilliant wooden staircase and a billiards board. There is a nice seating arrangement. There is a fireplace in the study as well. The Great bathroom looks alluring. A well-groomed kitchen lends an air of modernity to this quaint residence.

An adjacent wide dining space heightens its beauty. A butler’s pantry is just around the corner. The wide terrace overlooks the seventh fairway of the Biltmore Forest Country Club.

Andie has sold this property for $3 Million in May 2012. Someone very lucky is the new resident.
