Who Owns Casamigos Tequila? Casamigos Tequila Company History

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Casamigos Tequila, a renowned American tequila brand, has captivated the taste buds of tequila enthusiasts worldwide. Founded in 2013, the company has quickly become a force to be reckoned with in the spirits industry. But who owns Casamigos Tequila, and what is the story behind this exceptional brand?

Casamigos Tequila was founded by none other than George Clooney, the legendary Hollywood actor, along with Rande Gerber and Mike Meldman. Their passion for tequila led them to create a top-notch brand that would redefine the tequila experience.

From the very beginning, Casamigos had a clear vision – to produce high-quality tequila that was both affordable and inclusive. They wanted to break away from the exclusivity often associated with premium tequilas and offer a tequila that could be enjoyed by everyone. The founders named their brand “Casamigos,” which translates to “house of friends” in Spanish, reflecting their intention to create a tequila for personal use among their close group.

Impressively, Casamigos Tequila experienced immense growth and quickly became one of the fastest-growing spirits brands. The brand’s success could be attributed not only to its remarkable product but also to the involvement of George Clooney, who brought his star power and passion to the world of tequila.

The Founding of Casamigos Tequila

Casamigos Tequila was founded by George Clooney, Rande Gerber, and Mike Meldman. The idea for the tequila brand began during a vacation in Mexico, where the three friends decided to create their own tequila that they could enjoy without getting a hangover. They spent two years researching and developing the blend and two more years tasting and refining it. The founders wanted a tequila that was smooth, had the right flavor profile, and could be consumed straight or on the rocks. Their personal passion for tequila led to the creation of Casamigos.

“We wanted something that we could drink straight, that was really smooth and didn’t burn going down. That’s how we came to the decision that our tequila was perfect.” – George Clooney

The Acquisition by Diageo

In 2017, Diageo, a multinational beverage company, made a groundbreaking move by acquiring Casamigos Tequila in a deal worth $1 billion. This acquisition marked a significant milestone for both Diageo and Casamigos.

Diageo recognized the immense popularity and potential of Casamigos Tequila, which had rapidly gained a loyal following and established itself as a leading brand in the tequila market. The acquisition presented Diageo with an opportunity to enhance its already impressive portfolio by adding a high-profile tequila brand.

The deal involved an initial payment of $700 million to acquire Casamigos, with an additional $300 million contingent upon the performance of the brand over the next ten years. This bold investment reflected Diageo’s belief in the future success and value of Casamigos as a premium tequila brand.

By bringing Casamigos Tequila under its corporate umbrella, Diageo aimed to leverage the brand’s strong reputation and consumer appeal to further solidify its position in the spirits industry. This strategic move allowed Diageo to tap into the growing demand for high-quality tequila and capitalize on Casamigos’ remarkable success.

The Process of Making Casamigos Tequila

Casamigos Tequila prides itself on its meticulous production process, which ensures the creation of high-quality and consistent tequila. The brand’s commitment to excellence is evident in every step of its production, from the selection of premium ingredients to the careful aging and fermentation of the tequila.

To craft Casamigos Tequila, the brand uses 100% Blue Weber agave sourced from Jalisco, Mexico. The agave piñas, the heart of the agave plant, are carefully roasted in traditional ovens for 72 hours. This slow-roasting process allows the piñas to develop rich and complex flavors, resulting in a tequila that embodies the true essence of the agave plant.

One of the key factors that sets Casamigos Tequila apart is its proprietary yeast strain used during fermentation. This unique yeast strain ferments the agave juice for 80 hours, allowing for a refined and consistent flavor profile in every batch of tequila produced. The fermentation process is a critical step in developing the distinct taste and characteristics that Casamigos is known for.

After fermentation, Casamigos Tequila goes through an extensive aging or resting process, surpassing industry standards. The tequila is aged in oak barrels for longer periods, allowing it to develop a smooth and balanced taste. The aging process adds depth and complexity to the tequila’s flavor, resulting in a premium product cherished by tequila connoisseurs.

This entire production process is overseen by a Master Distiller, who ensures that each bottle of Casamigos Tequila meets the brand’s stringent quality standards. Every detail, from ingredient selection to aging duration, is carefully managed to deliver an exceptional tequila experience.

The Range of Casamigos Tequila Products

Casamigos Tequila offers a diverse range of products designed to cater to different preferences and taste profiles. With a commitment to quality and craftsmanship, each tequila and mezcal in the Casamigos portfolio showcases the brand’s dedication to providing a premium tequila experience.

Blanco Tequila

The blanco tequila from Casamigos is an unaged spirit that is known for its clean and crisp flavor. Crafted from 100% Blue Weber agave, this tequila offers a smooth and refreshing taste that is perfect for sipping on its own or as the base for cocktails. The blanco tequila exemplifies the brand’s commitment to producing high-quality tequila with a distinct character.

Reposado Tequila

The reposado tequila is aged for a minimum of two months, allowing the flavors to develop and mellow. This aging process gives the tequila a smooth and slightly oaky taste, with hints of vanilla and caramel. The reposado tequila from Casamigos is a crowd-pleaser that can be enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails. It is a testament to the brand’s dedication to creating tequila that is both approachable and sophisticated.

Añejo Tequila

For those seeking a more complex and mature flavor profile, Casamigos offers an añejo tequila that is aged for a minimum of one year. This extended aging process results in a tequila with rich and nuanced flavors, including notes of oak, chocolate, and spices. The añejo tequila is meant to be savored slowly, allowing the flavors to unfold and develop on the palate. It represents the pinnacle of Casamigos’ tequila craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Mezcal

In addition to its range of tequilas, Casamigos has also introduced a mezcal to its lineup. Mezcal is a smoky and earthy spirit that is distilled from different varieties of agave. The Casamigos mezcal offers a unique taste experience, with its distinct smoky flavor and hints of fruit and spice. It is a bold and adventurous addition to the brand’s portfolio, showcasing their willingness to explore different flavors and styles within the agave spirits category.

The wide range of Casamigos Tequila products ensures that there is something for every tequila enthusiast, from those who appreciate the clean and crisp flavors of blanco tequila to those who prefer the complexity of añejo tequila or the boldness of mezcal. Each product exemplifies the brand’s dedication to producing high-quality tequila and mezcal that can be enjoyed and appreciated by all.
