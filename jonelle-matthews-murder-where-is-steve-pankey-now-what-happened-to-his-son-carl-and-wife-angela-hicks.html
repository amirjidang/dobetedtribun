Steve Pankey Wikipedia: Where Is Jonelle Matthews Murder Accused Now?

Despite being the name of one of the most talked-about cases, Jonelle Matthews’ murder accused Steve Pankey is yet to land on Wikipedia. However, he has been mentioned on Jonelle Matthews’ Murder page.

He is a 70-year-old who had been diagnosed with Asperger’s syndrome and autism. Before October 2021, the accused was still in prison and the case was only delayed, ruling on admissible evidence.
