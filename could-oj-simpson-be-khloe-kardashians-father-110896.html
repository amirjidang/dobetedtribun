Could O.J. Simpson Be Khloe Kardashians Father?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

The rumor mill is going crazy with allegations that O.J. Simpson is claiming to be the biological father of Khloe Kardashian—even going to the length of putting her in his will.

In this bizarre report, The National Enquirer suggests that a source confirmed O.J.’s belief that Khloe was birthed through an affair with Kris Jenner while she was still married to her now-deceased husband Robert Kardashian.

As far as his will—which is said to be in the millions and expected to grow—O.J. allegedly has plans to add Khloe into it, while replacing his eldest daughter Arnelle. Reports say she allegedly mishandled his money, leading to the impending foreclosure of his four-bedroom Miami-area home.

His other three children are said to be not happy about this, at all. A source tells the Enquirer, “They think he is a real jerk for taking out everything on Ar­nelle, who uprooted her life in Los Ange­les to move to Florida to take care of him. By cutting Arnelle out of his will, his kids feel O.J. is being cruel. And they think that adding Khloe is just plain revenge.”

Not too sure about this one, given the source. However, this would be explosive if it’s actually true. We hope neither family gets torn apart from these allegations.
