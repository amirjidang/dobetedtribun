Celebs Who Lived With Their Parents Up Until Recently

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

When we look at celebrities, we instantly assume they are rolling in dough, living lavishly in their mansions, and enjoying life totally autonomously. While that might be the case for some big names in the biz, there are a few celebs who aren't about that solo life.

In 2015, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West moved back in with momma bear and manager, Kris Jenner for not one, not two, but nearly three years as their home was under construction. Despite being amongst the richest and most famous people in the world, they still went back home to live with their parents! Well, they aren't the only celebs who have done so.

Related: Does Olivia Rodrigo Still Live With Her Parents?

Pete Davidson notoriously lived in his mother's basement for years before getting a place of his own, and we don't blame him for doing it one bit! So, which other celebs lived with their parents despite being rich and famous? Let's dive in!

9 Pete Davidson

Pete Davidson has been living large ever since he gained prominence in the comedy scene. Following his success on Saturday Night Live, Davidson was all anyone could talk about – that and his split from pop icon, Ariana Grande, of course. Well, it seems as if Pete made headlines when he revealed that he still lived with his mother in Staten Island, despite being worth millions.

Turns out, the duo bought a house together in their New York neighborhood, where Pete occupied the entire basement. Although their setup was stellar, and Pete's relationship with his mom is nothing short of amazing, the actor and comedian ultimately pulled the plug and officially get a place of his own following the release of his 2020 film, The King Of Staten Island.

8 Jennifer Lawrence

Jennifer Lawrence is without a doubt one of the biggest names in Hollywood, so what was she doing living with her parents for so long? During her interview on The Ellen Show, Jennifer, who was an Oscar winner at the time, mentioned that she still lived with her mom.

The Hunger Games star went on to reveal that during her time living at home, her Oscar voting ballot, which all Academy members receive, ended up in her mom's hands, leading her to cast the vote instead! Luckily for Lawrence, she doesn't have to worry about that anymore after having moved out on her own.

7 Zendaya

Zendaya is on the rise and we love to see it! The Emmy winner has been crushing the game when it comes to her performance in Euphoria, which has easily made her millions. While she's on her own now, Zendaya recently lived with her parents, however, she didn't mind it in the slightest.

Despite her fame and fortune, the actress shared with Ellen DeGeneres that having mom and dad under the same roof has its perks, which include mom being a human alarm clock, and of course, having meals ready at any given moment. The star also discussed the boundaries that they had in place, which made for a much easier living situation.

6 Addison Rae

Addison Rae has been on the rise ever since her success on TikTok. The star released her very first video back in July of 2019 and is now one of the biggest names from the app. During her visit to the Unfiltered podcast, Addison revealed that despite her online fame, she remained at home in Louisana where her parents continued to support her dreams of becoming an entertainer.

Related: Addison Rae Opens Up About Moving To Los Angeles

Well, it didn't take much time before she left college and Louisiana for Los Angeles, which is where she currently resides. Luckily for Addison, she's made new family and friends here, especially with fellow gal pal Kourtney Kardashian.

5 Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish revealed that when it comes to her living situation, she might not ever be leaving home! Despite being one of the biggest names in music, Eilish still lives with her parents, which is understandable considering she is only 19 years old.

Well, turns out that she not only lives with her parents but sometimes sleeps in their bed! Billie shared that she suffers from certain sleep disorders that impact her sleep schedule, so sleeping alongside mom and dad provides her with the security and protection needed to get a good night's rest.

4 Michael B. Jordan

Michael B. Jordan may have made a name for himself in Creed and as a member of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, however, that doesn't mean he's about that solo living life.

In 2016, Jordan purchased a home for him and his parents in Oakbank, California, and while he got a lot of questions regarding his living situation, he made it clear that it was his dream to be able to do this for his mom and dad. While they lived together for a few years, the actor is no longer roommates with his parents, especially now that he is in a serious relationship with Lori Harvey.

3 Peter Weber

Although Peter might be one of the most popular names to come out of Bachelor Nation, that doesn't mean he's not a momma's boy! The former Bachelor revealed that he lived with his parents up until his newfound fame, and a little after, too!

Related: 10 Lesser-Known Facts About 'Bachelor' Star, Peter Weber

Considering his job, which is being a pilot, requires him to be gone a lot, there was no purpose for him to have a place of his own – that is, until he found "the one".

2 Amanda Bynes

Amanda Bynes was the "it" actor of the 2000s, however, things took a turn following a slew of mental health issues stemming from her rough upbringing in the spotlight. Bynes remains under a conservatorship, which is controlled by her parents, whom she lived with for years after her downward spiral.

While this situation is not one that Amanda loves, so much that she has called out for help on social media, it appears as if things are much better. Bynes, whose conservatorship has been extended to 2023, has slightly more autonomy now, so much that she even got engaged!

1 Spencer And Heidi

Heidi Montag and Spencer Pratt were on top of the world following their success on MTV's hit series, The Hills. Despite making millions, and having a collective net worth of $10 million to be exact, the duo blew through all of their money, and as of 2012, they were broke.

Speidi then moved into Spencer's parent's home, as they couldn't afford to live the lavish lifestyle they once did. Luckily for the two, The Hills: New Beginnings came about, allowing Speidi to earn money consistently, which has allowed them to move out and live in their own condo within the Hollywood Hills.
