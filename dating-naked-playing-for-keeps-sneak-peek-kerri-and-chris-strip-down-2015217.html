Dating Naked Playing for Keeps Sneak Peek: Kerri and Chris Strip Down

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Stripping down! Season 2 of VH1’s Dating Naked, called Playing for Keeps, follows two daters who will look for love over a full season — naturally, they’ll be in the nude.

Us Weekly has an exclusive sneak peek at the premiere, in which contestants Kerri Cipriani and Chris Aldrich strip down as they begin their search for a soulmate.

“It’s so awkward, but at the same time, let’s be honest: When is the last time that a woman took off her clothes and a guy was like, ‘Actually, I don’t want to have sex with you.’ That doesn’t happen,” Kerri points out.

Of course, things are still awkward when she takes her clothes off. “Oh s—, I am naked! Everything is in the light of day,” she reflects.

Chris is hesitant from the get-go. “I was nervous about being naked. My ass has never seen the light of day,” he cracks.
