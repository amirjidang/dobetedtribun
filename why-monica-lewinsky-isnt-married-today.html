The Real Reason Monica Lewinsky Isn't Married Today

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Highlights

  • Monica Lewinsky faced intense public scrutiny and humiliation during the Bill Clinton scandal, being unfairly blamed for the affair.
  • Despite societal pressure to get married, Lewinsky chooses to keep her personal life private and recognizes the importance of finding the right partner.
  • Lewinsky has learned from past relationships and is more accepting of the possibility that she may never get married, focusing on her own happiness and not succumbing to public pressure.

If you asked a lot of people to list the biggest controversies of all time, they would instinctively think to talk about some of the scandals that have rocked Hollywood. While it is true those scandals tend to garner a lot of attention, there have been some controversies in the past that had much more serious implications which everyone was talking about.

Over the last several decades, every American President has been constantly attacked by their political enemies. When President Bill Clinton was in office, a lot of the attacks against him revolved around his marriage to Hillary Clinton right from the start.

Unfortunately for Monica Lewinsky, she got wrapped up in that whirlwind and thrust into the public eye. Thankfully that scandal faded away years ago, but now some onlookers are wondering something else about Lewinsky, why isn’t she married?

The Public’s Changing Perspective Of Monica Lewinsky

In January of 1998, the world learned that the President of the United States at the time, Bill Clinton, had been carrying on an affair with a young woman named Monica Lewinsky. Employed as a White House intern at the time of her involvement with Clinton, when the world learned Lewinsky’s name she suddenly was thrust under an indescribably harsh spotlight.

At the height of the Bill Clinton and Monica Lewinsky scandal, it seemed like everyone in the media had something extremely nasty to say about her. Never mind the fact that Bill was the one who was cheating on his wife and the power differential between them was massive, lots of people decided Lewsinky was the one to be blamed for the affair.

Many years after things finally calmed down, Monica Lewinsky wrote about what she went through and revealed she felt like “the most humiliated woman in the world”. Looking back on the coverage, it is easy to see why she felt that way.

On the bright side, even though some observers still harshly judge her, a lot of people agree with Lewinsky’s belief that Bill Clinton should want to apologize to her.

Why Monica Lewinsky Hasn’t Gotten Married Yet

In decades past, women were expected to get married at a young age and if they didn’t, everyone assumed there was something seriously wrong with them. Thankfully, society has advanced a lot since then. However, if anyone thought that women no longer were pressured to walk down the aisle, they haven’t been paying attention.

Unfortunately, there are a lot of regular people and stars who’ve gotten married and divorced over and over again. With that in mind, you’d think that everyone would understand that trying to push people to get married no matter what is a mistake.

Despite that, lots of women above a certain age find themselves getting asked one question during every family reunion, when are you going to get married?

Monica Lewinski Chooses To Keep Her Personal Life Private

Even though society has progressed enough that people now know that Monica Lewinsky’s past treatment was horrendous, things are still pretty messed up. For proof of that, all you have to do is look at the fact that when Monica Lewinsky spoke to People in 2021, she was asked about the state of her love life today.

Even though it seems backward to ask a woman about her dating life after she was raked over the coals for years over a relationship, Monica Lewinsky handled the inquiry well. However, that doesn’t mean that Lewinsky was willing to gossip about her love life with People’s interviewer.

"I kinda feel if anybody has earned a right to have their romantic life private, it's me. Those relationships are very precious to me, even the one or two who turned out to be putzes. But I've learned a lot."

After stating that she wasn’t willing to talk about her relationships pubically, Monica Lewinsky went on to address the fact that she has never been married. "I do date. I'm not married yet. I don't know if that will happen or not, and I'm more okay with that than I used to be."

Of course, Monica Lewinsky didn’t specifically state why she hasn’t gotten married in either of those quotes. However, it could easily be argued that both of those statements actually explain why Lewinsky hasn’t walked down the aisle.

As the first quote reveals, Monica Lewinsky is perfectly willing to admit that in her opinion, some of the people she has been involved with turned out to be “putzes”. It also is worth noting that even though she previously found it hard to accept the idea that she may never get married, she is beginning to come to terms with that.

When you look at those two concepts together, it certainly seems like Lewinsky is saying she hasn’t found the right partner yet and she has figured out that getting married to anyone is a bad idea. For someone who was pubically shamed for years, it is great to see that she seems to be doing a good job of not bowing to public pressure about her personal life.
