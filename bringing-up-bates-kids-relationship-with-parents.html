What The 'Bringing Up Bates' Kids Really Thought Of Their Parents

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 12, 2024

Bringing Up Bates' fans were ecstatic with the news of the show's renewal for its 11th installment, which was previously scheduled to air in February 2022. Unfortunately, UPtv has taken the show off from its slot, and it will not air as eventually planned. Still, the Bates family has had a really busy and momentous year with many Bates children walking down the aisle and several little ones joining their large brood. There are lots of important and exciting family moments to catch up on. Here is what all the 19 Bates children have been up to and what they really think of their parents.

15 What Zachary Bates Really Thinks Of His Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

The eldest child of Gil and Kelly Bates, Zach, married Whitney Perkins in December 2013. The pair have four kids: Bradley, Kaci, Khlo√©, and Jadon. Their youngest son was born last June. Zach is a family man and has a good relationship with his parents.

14 What Michaela Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Michaela, who was born Michael, expressed her distaste for having a traditionally male name. Hence, her family gave her the name Michaela. As her parents respect her decisions, she gets along with them. The oldest Bates daughter tied the knot with Brandon Keilen in 2015.

13 What Erin Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Erin and her husband of eight years, Chad Paine, are proud parents of son Carson and three daughters: Brooklyn, Everly, and Holland. Shortly after the birth of her fourth child, the reality star began suffering from serious medical conditions, which led to her having multiple surgeries in 2020. Thankfully, her parents have always been there for her. When the couple discovered Erin was pregnant with her fifth child, she and Chad were in disbelief. Erin gave birth to her miracle baby girl Finley Marie on January 18, 2022.

12 What Lawson Bates Really Thinks Of His Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Lawson Bates works as a reserve deputy for Anderson County's Sheriff's Department. In early 2021, he went public with his relationship with Tiffany Espensen. The couple got engaged on October 26, 2021, after Lawson popped the question in Italy. The couple was happy to have support from Lawson's parents.

11 What Nathan Bates Really Thinks Of His Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

The commercial pilot and flight instructor proposed to his girlfriend Esther Keyes in May 2021, and the pair eventually tied the knot five months later. Their wedding was a grand affair, with 350 guests attending the ceremony. In the pictures, Nathan seems very close to Gil and Kelly.

10 What Alyssa Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

After marrying John Webster in 2014, Alyssa went on to welcome daughters Allie, Lexi, and Zoey. The pair announced their fourth pregnancy in August 2020, and their daughter Maci was born six months later. Alyssa loves her family, especially her parents.

9 What Tori Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Tori and her husband of four years, Bobby Smith, share two sons, Kade and Kolter. The pair's first daughter and third child Charlotte Raine was born in June 2021. The infant had a major health issue because her umbilical cord was wrapped around her neck so tightly that her oxygen levels were affected. But she managed to pull through the ordeal and has lived a healthy life. Gil and Kelly were there for Alyssa in such a difficult time, and she feels grateful for it.

8 What Trace Bates Really Thinks Of His Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Trace, a police officer by profession, also enjoys numerous adventurous sports like skydiving and scuba diving. The 24-year-old has over 100,000 Instagram followers and loves giving an intimate peek into his private life. Currently, he is in a serious relationship with Lydia Romeike. He seems to have a good relationship with Gil and Kelly.

7 What Carlin And Josie Bates Really Think Of Their Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Carlin married her husband Evan Stewart in May 2019, and the duo welcomed their daughter Layla less than a year later. The couple's newborn was diagnosed with a heart condition. Thankfully, Carlin's parents helped her to take care of the baby. Carlin is expecting a baby boy later in August this year.

Josie and her husband Kelton Balka welcomed daughter Willow in July 2019. Nine months after their wedding, their second daughter and rainbow baby Hazel Sloane was born exactly two years later. Josie loves visiting her parent's house.

6 What Katie And Jackson Bates Really Think Of Their Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Katie began courting Travis Clark the same year she graduated from Crown College. She married on December 3 at Castleton Farms in Loudon, Tennessee, the same venue where her sister Carlin got married. Katie loves to spend time with Gil and Kelly.

Jackson graduated from high school in May 2020. Last February, he underwent surgery after a snowboarding accident during a family vacation to Colorado. Gil and Kelly have been taking care of him.

5 What Warden And Isaiah Bates Really Think Of Their Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Warden is said to be a natural athlete, a great singer, and a hard worker. His family calls him Tigger because he's full of energy. There's no doubt of Warden's strong bond with Gil and Kelly. The teenager graduated from high school last year and currently works at his brother-in-law Chad's company.

Described by his mother as a spiritual-minded, thoughtful, and hard-working person, Isaiah is the 14th child of the Bates household. He also holds the accolade of being the tallest member of the Bates family.

4 What Addallee Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Addallee, who was born prematurely in 2006, suffers from hearing loss and has to rely on hearing aids. Termed as an angel by her parents, the teenager is said to be a thoughtful person who always prioritizes her family before herself.

3 What Ellie Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Just like her name, which signifies strong light is truly the light of the Bates family. The tender-hearted and sensitive teenager loves to give hugs and cuddles to her family members, especially Gil and Kelly.

2 What Callie Bates Really Thinks Of Her Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Callie is the youngest daughter in the Bates family. She is considered a sassy and bossy child who is unafraid to speak her mind. Callie has a good relationship with her parents.

1 What Judson And Jeb Bates Really Think Of Their Parents Gil & Kelly Jo Bates

Judson is said to be a very calm and sweet child. He is currently being homeschooled along with the other younger Bates children. In pictures with his parents, he looks really happy.

The youngest child of the family, Jeb, is highly energetic and fearless. However, most of his siblings feel that he is very spoiled by Gil and Kelly.
