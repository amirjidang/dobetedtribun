These Are Anya Taylor-Joy's Most Profitable Movies

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 26, 2024

Actress Anya Taylor-Joy is surely best known for starring in the Netflix hit The Queen's Gambit. However, the 25-year-old has definitely appeared in some other memorable projects and currently, she is rumored to be joining the Marvel Cinematic Universe, she will be the voice behind Princess Peach in the upcoming Super Mario Bros movie, and she is set to star in an upcoming project alongside Taylor Swift and Margot Robbie.

Today, we're focusing on the movies the actress has appeared in. From Last Night in Soho to The New Mutants — keep on scrolling to find out just which of Anya Taylor-Joy's movies made the most at the box office!

10 'Radioactive' — Box Office: $3.5 Million

Kicking the list off is the 2019 biographical drama Radioactive. In it, Anya Taylor-Joy plays Irene Curie, and she stars alongside Rosamund Pike, Sam Riley, and Aneurin Barnard. The movie tells the story of Marie Sklodowska-Curie and her Nobel Prize-winning work — and it currently has a 6.3 rating on IMDb. Radioactive ended up making $3.5 million at the box office.

9 'Morgan' — Box Office: $8.8 Million

Next on the list is the 2016 sci-fi thriller movie Morgan in which Anya Taylor-Joy plays the titular character. Besides the actress, the movie also stars Kate Mara, Toby Jones, Rose Leslie, Boyd Holbrook, and Michelle Yeoh. Morgan follows a corporate consultant who is trying to decide whether to terminate an artificially created humanoid — and it currently has a 5.8 rating on IMDb. The movie ended up making $8.8 million at the box office.

8 'Marrowbone' — Box Office: $12.2 Million

Let's move on to the 2017 psychological horror mystery Marrowbone. In it, Anya Taylor-Joy portrays Allie, and she stars alongside George MacKay, Charlie Heaton, Mia Goth, Matthew Stagg, and Kyle Soller.

The movie follows the Marrowbone siblings as they move into their old family manor, and it currently has a 6.7 rating on IMDb. Marrowbone ended up making $12.2 million at the box office.

7 'Vampire Academy' — Box Office: $15.4 Million

The 2014 fantasy horror-comedy Vampire Academy is next. In it, Anya Taylor-Joy plays the Feeder Girl, and she stars alongside Zoey Deutch, Lucy Fry, Danila Kozlovsky, Dominic Sherwood, and Cameron Monaghan. The movie is based on the first book of the novel series of the same name by Richelle Mead — and it currently has a 5.5 rating on IMDb. Vampire Academy ended up making $15.4 million at the box office.

6 'Last Night In Soho' — Box Office: $23.1 Million

Next on the list is the 2021 psychological horror Last Night in Soho in which Anya Taylor-Joy plays Sandie. The movie also stars Thomasin McKenzie, Matt Smith, Michael Ajao, Terence Stamp, and Diana Rigg — and it currently has a 7.2 rating on IMDb. Last Night in Soho follows an aspiring fashion designer who manages to enter the 1960s in her dreams, and it ended up making $23.1 million at the box office.

5 'Emma' — Box Office: $26 Million

Opening up the top five on today's list is the 2020 romantic period comedy Emma. In it, Anya Taylor-Joy plays Emma Woodhouse, and she stars alongside Johnny Flynn, Josh O'Connor, Callum Turner, Mia Goth, and Miranda Hart. The movie is based on Jane Austen's 1815 novel of the same name, and it currently has a 6.7 rating on IMDb. Emma ended up making $26 million at the box office.

4 'The Witch' — Box Office: $40.4 Million

Let's move on to the 2015 horror movie The Witch in which Anya Taylor-Joy plays Thomasin. The movie also stars Ralph Ineson, Kate Dickie, Harvey Scrimshaw, Ellie Grainger, and Lucas Dawson — and it follows a family in the 1630s who encounter forces of evil behind their farm.

The Witch currently has a 6.9 rating on IMDb, and it ended up making $40.4 million at the box office.

3 'The New Mutants' — Box Office: $49.1 Million

Opening up the top three on today's list is the 2020 superhero horror movie The New Mutants. In it, Anya Taylor-Joy plays Illyana Rasputin / Magik, and she stars alongside Maisie Williams, Charlie Heaton, Alice Braga, Blu Hunt, and Henry Zaga. The is based on the Marvel Comics team of the same name, and it currently has a 5.3 rating on IMDb. The New Mutants ended up making $49.1 million at the box office.

2 'Glass' — Box Office: $247 Million

The runner-up on today's list is the 2019 superhero movie Glass in which Anya Taylor-Joy portrays Casey Cooke. The movie also stars James McAvoy, Bruce Willis, Sarah Paulson, and Samuel L. Jackson, and it currently has a 6.6 rating on IMDb. Glass is the third installment in the Unbreakable trilogy, and it ended up making $247 million at the box office.

1 'Split' — Box Office: $278.5 Million

And finally, wrapping the list up at spot number one is the 2016 psychological horror Split. In it, Anya Taylor-Joy also plays Casey Cooke, and she stars alongside James McAvoy and Betty Buckley. Split is a sequel to the 2000 movie Unbreakable, and it currently has a 7.3 rating on IMDb. The movie ended up making $278.5 million at the box office.
