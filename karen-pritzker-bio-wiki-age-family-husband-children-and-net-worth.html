Karen Pritzker Foundation

Pritzker works as president as well as the director of The Seedlings Foundation. The Seedlings Foundation, founded in the year 2002, has awarded millions of dollars in grants, catalyzing advancements in medical research, social services, job retraining for adults, affordable housing as well as online news sites dedicated to local, factual, ad-free reporting.

Pritzker and her husband donated $20 million to the Yale University School of Medicine, including $3 million to endow a professorship; $5 million to Teach for America; $1.5 million to the Michael J.
