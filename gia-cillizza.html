Gia Cillizza is famous for being the wife of Chris Cillizza. Chris is a political commentator for the television news channel, CNN. Moreover, Gia is also a head field hockey coach for CUA. 

In hockey, she started her career in 2002 as a head coach. She has worked as an assistant coach for 3 years before that. She is one of the greatest hockey coaches to coach a catholic school. However, she has also gained widespread fame through her husband’s reputation.
