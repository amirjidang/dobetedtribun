Meet Nick Offermans Wife, Megan Mullally Where Is She Now?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Nick Offerman’s wife, Megan Mullally is an American actress, comedian, and singer. She is best known for playing Karen Walker on the NBC sitcom Will & Grace.

Nick Offerman and Megan Mullally have been married since 2003, which adds evidence to their being a perfect comedy match. 

Both Offerman and Mullally have had successful acting careers on classic NBC sitcoms, each playing iconic characters throughout their time on television. 

Despite the age difference, they fit together perfectly, both personally and professionally. 

Nick and Megan have embraced their relationship outside of the camera; they even presented the 37th Film Independent Spirit Awards in 2022 as a joint-hosting team—a first for husband and wife hosts! 

They have been acting together ever since, proving that true love has no bounds despite gaps in age or career achievements. Nick Offerman and Megan Mullally are an undeniable success story of Hollywood proportions.

Offerman’s performance as Bill in HBO’s The Last of Us is an exciting one to behold.

Whereas the game portrayed Bill as an over-the-top character, the series dives deeper into his story and uncovers the conflict between him, Frank, and what lies beyond those walls protecting his neighborhood.

In this more intimate version of him, we even see a friendship with Frank that wouldn’t have been possible in the game due to other circumstances. 

The showrunner consciously decided to use Offerman’s talents to create a compelling and nuanced character that viewers are sure to love.

Who is Nick Offerman’s wife?

Megan Mullally is a highly accomplished actor, comedian, and singer born in Los Angeles, California. 

After moving to Oklahoma City at the age of six, she began studying ballet and performing at the Oklahoma City Ballet while juggling studies at the School of American Ballet in New York. 

She attended college at Northwestern University and was already acting in eight shows per week by junior year before ultimately deciding to drop out in pursuit of her performing career. 

She also has roots in England, Ireland, and Scandinavia, as evidenced by her English, Irish, and Scandinavian ancestry.

Nick Offerman and Megan Mullally are an incomparable couple that has been married since 2003, but meeting each other over two decades ago was not love at first sight. 

In 2000, they went to the same Evidence Room Theatre Company production of The Berlin Circle, where Mullally claims that Offerman’s back hair distracted her from his adorably humble look. 

He was wearing gold overalls because he couldn’t afford a notebook! It’s wild to think that this silly outfit–and bad haircare–helped kickstart one of Hollywood’s longest-lasting relationships.
