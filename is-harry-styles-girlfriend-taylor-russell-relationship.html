Over the years, Harry Styles has captivated not only the music industry but also the gossip columns with his relationships. From high-profile romances to rumored flings, the former One Direction heartthrob has left a trail of curiosity and speculation in his wake.

On the other hand, Taylor Russell made her first million on breakout role in the Netflix sci-fi hit series Lost in Space. However, in the 2019 drama film Waves, Russell truly showcased her exceptional talent. In this emotionally charged movie, Russell's portrayal of Emily, the daughter at the heart of the story, garnered widespread acclaim. Her portrayal resonated deeply with audiences and critics alike, her remarkable performance was recognized when she clinched the coveted Gotham Independent Film Award for Breakthrough Actor.

Recently, Styles and Russell have been the subject of dating rumors which left fans baffled and intrigued whether there is any truth to any of it.

Are Harry Styles And Taylor Russell Dating?

Reports have emerged that Harry Styles was seen on a potential date with popular Canadian actress Taylor Russell, causing a frenzy among his fans. Russell, known for her impressive performances in significant films, was spotted taking a leisurely walk with Styles.

The sighting occurred on June 23, 2023, as the 28-year-old actress and 29-year-old singer were leaving a contemporary art gallery in London, according to AceShowbiz. Russell looked fashionable in a white top, while Styles sported a navy blue jacket and black pants that complemented her outfit. The leaked images have ignited a wave of excitement among Styles’ fans on social media.

Numerous Reddit users have shared their thoughts on these dating rumors. One user expressed skepticism, stating, "I don't think they're necessarily friends or a couple. It doesn't have to be one or the other. They're probably not even acquaintances."

Who Has Taylor Russell Dated?

Taylor Russell has quickly risen to stardom in film and television. She has garnered attention for her standout performances in movies such as Bones and All and her role in the series Lost In Space.

Lucas is widely recognized for his remarkable work in acclaimed films such as Manchester by the Sea, Boy Erased, and Lady Bird.

Russell and Hedges share the screen in their 2019 movie Waves, directed by Trey Edward Shults. Their relationship became public knowledge in December 2018 when they were caught kissing at a New York Knicks basketball game.

Taylor Russell was also subjected to dating rumors with her co-star, Timothée Chalamet, since the premiere of Bones and All at the Venice Film Festival in September 2022. Throughout their press tour, observant viewers have taken note of the couple's affectionate behavior—whispering, hugging, and displaying a playful closeness.

Chalamet and Russell have captivated fans with their undeniable on-screen chemistry and fashionable red-carpet appearances which made fans believe something was going on between them.
