Alexa Bliss vs. Shayna Baszler Announced For WWE Hell In A Cell, Updated Card

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 19, 2024

Following the controversial show-closing angle to last week’s episode of WWE RAW, Alexa Bliss will face Shayna Baszler at the Hell in a Cell pay-per-view on Sunday. Bliss and her doll, Lilly have tormented Baszler in recent weeks, and the conflict will come to a head at WWE Hell in a Cell.

The full announcement from WWE’s official website is as follows:

At WWE Hell in a Cell, the twisted Alexa Bliss will surely have Lilly lurking in the shadows when she goes one-on-one with an irate Shayna Baszler hellbent on teaching Little Miss Bliss a lesson.

For weeks, sommelier Reginald was the victim of a series of mysterious events while in the corner of Baszler and Nia Jax during their matches. The Queen of Spaces eventually set the blame for these strange occurrences, as well as for her dominant tandem losing the WWE Women’s Tag Team Titles, at Bliss’ feet. She even went so far as to journey onto Alexa’s Playground, beat up Reggie and dismiss Alexa’s “friend” Lilly as being a “stupid little doll.”

After Baszler stomped Lilly on the ground the following week, she was met with a series of flames that chased her to the backstage area. When she barricaded herself in a dressing room, she found Lilly staring back at her in the mirror. This made a pay-per-view showdown inevitable.

Don’t miss the horrifying showdown this Sunday, streaming at 8 ET/5 PT on Peacock in the United States and WWE Network everywhere else.

Otherwise, the card for the pay-per-view is as follows:

Hell in a Cell Match for the WWE Championship: Bobby Lashley (c) vs. Drew McIntyre

Hell in a Cell Match for the WWE Universal Championship: Roman Reigns (c) vs. Rey Mysterio

SmackDown Women’s Championship: Bianca Belair (c) vs. Bayley

RAW Women’s Championship: Rhea Ripley (c) vs. Charlotte Flair
