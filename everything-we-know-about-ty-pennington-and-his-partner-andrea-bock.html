Is Ty Pennington Gay? Who Is His Partner?

No, Ty Pennington is not gay. He is a straight man.

Ty is currently in a longtime relationship with his girlfriend Andrea Bock, who is also a cast of Extreme Makeover: Home Edition.

Andrea Bock and Ty Pennington knew each other since the early age of 11. They have been together for more than 18 years but still don’t see any need for getting married.

Currently, Andrea is Ty Pennington’s manager and is very supportive of him. Prior to becoming his manager, she worked as a manager in the show business.

Ty Pennington Age, Family

Ty Pennington was born on October 19, 1964, in Atlanta, Georgia. He is currently at the age of 56.

Speaking about his family, he was born to his mother Yvonne Burton, a school psychologist. He also has an older brother Wynn Pennington.

Ty’s mother separated from his biological father when both Wynn and Ty were very young. Prior to getting remarried, she spent many years as a single mother. Ty and Wynn adopted their step-father’s name.

At just the age of 17, Ty Pennington was diagnosed with Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), which is a neurodevelopment disorder. Later, he started raising awareness for the treatment of adult ADHD.

Ty attended Sprayberry High School located in Georgia. For his university, he studied art and history at Kennesaw State University. Later, he graduated with a degree in graphic design from the Art Institute of Atlanta.

Moreover, Ty also became the author of his book Good Design Can Change Your Life: Beautiful Rooms, Inspiring Stories, as well as Ty’s Tricks: Home Repair Secrets Plus Cheap and Easy Projects to Transform Any Room.
