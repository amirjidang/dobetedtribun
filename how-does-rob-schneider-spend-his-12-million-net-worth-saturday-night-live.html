How Does Rob Schneider Spend His $12 Million Net Worth?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Rob Schneider started his career in entertainment during his high school days but it was not until the late ‘80s that he was fully launched into the industry when he was hired as a writer for Saturday Night Live. Gradually, he transitioned from writing to featuring as a full cast member on the show. On SNL, Schneider played different roles at different times but his most memorable character was Richard Laymer who was a fan favorite.

Related: Why Rob Schneider Turned Down This Adam Sandler Film

However, despite being best known for Saturday Night Live, Schneider has appeared in several other television shows and films. With his incredible work, this comic actor continues to make a name for himself across the entertainment industry. And while at it, he has also made a lot of money. Schneider is currently estimated to be worth about $12 million. But with his seemingly busy schedule, how does he spend all of this money? You’re about to find out.

9 Baseball Cards

Schneider is an avid baseball card collector and has a passion for collecting cards from the 1950s and 1960s. The talented actor and comedian collects rare baseball cards such as a 1951 Willie Mays baseball card estimated at $175,000 and many more similarly expensive cards.

8 Expensive Jewelry

In addition to his love for baseball cards, Schneider apparently also enjoys splurging on expensive jewelry. The ace director proposed to his current wife Patricia Azarcoya Arce with a diamond engagement ring worth thousands of dollars. Rob reportedly also spends a lot of money updating his watch collection which is filled with Cartier pieces.

7 Luxury Houses

Rob works hard for his money so it is no surprise that he enjoys spending it just as much. The actor often invests in luxury properties which he ends up selling at a profit. In 2009, Rob put his San Francisco French Normandy-style manor home up for sale at $3.6 million. He reportedly bought the property for a little over $1.9 million. In 2004 he sold his former LA home for $1.09 million. To date, Rob continues to buy luxurious properties and enjoys the high-end features they provide.

6 Luxury Cars

Like many celebrities, Rob also has a love for luxury cars and this is evident with his car collection. The comedian reportedly owns a convertible 2-seater sports car with state-of-the-art technology, a carbon fiber interior, and luxury leather seats. From his Instagram, it's not hard to see that this is one of Rob’s favorite cars. In addition to the convertible, the actor is also a proud owner of a silver Porsche Carerra cabriolet identical to the one he drove in Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo.

5 Private Weddings And Honeymoon

In April 2011, Rob tied the knot (for the third time) with television producer Patricia Azarcoya Arce. Though a low-key ceremony, there is no doubt that a lot of money went into making the couple’s big day a success. Following their wedding, Rob and Patricia went on a week-long honeymoon in Taiwan where they had a taste of the local cuisines, dined at the famous Din Tai Fung restaurant in Taipei, and enjoyed the exquisite vibe at the Shilin Night Market.

4 Vacations

All work and no break makes Rob a dull boy! The comic actor who doubles as a father to three lovely kids makes sure to take time off work and is no stranger to family vacations. The adorable family often make trips to different places around the world and keeps their fans updated on their fun moments via social media.

3 Clothes

Rob Schneider isn't your regular actor, and he doesn't do traditional clothes either. The renowned actor apparently has a love for vintage clothing and is the proud owner of a collection of Hawaiian shirts. With his outfits regularly taking us back to the ‘70s, we are almost convinced that Rob might be in the wrong style era.

2 Paintings

Rob loves paintings as much as he loves acting, and the comic actor has a treasured painting of Jack Lord of Hawaii Five-O (1968), which he first came across during a family vacation with his parents in Oahu when he was 8. Years later, he found the portrait in a vintage shop in San Francisco and bought it. Rob Schneider has a thing for vintage things, and he spends his money judiciously satisfying his desires.

1 Birthdays And Anniversaries

With three children and a wife, Rob undoubtedly spends a lot of money on his family. Special milestones like birthdays and anniversaries might even mean splurging more money. In addition to going all out to make his loved ones feel special on their big days, Rob warms hearts with social media tributes that leave fans raving.

At 57, Schneider is no doubt an accomplished entertainer in every sense of the word. And with how hard he has worked, it is only fitting that he spends his money on things that bring him true happiness. And from what we can tell, this legend has exquisite taste.
