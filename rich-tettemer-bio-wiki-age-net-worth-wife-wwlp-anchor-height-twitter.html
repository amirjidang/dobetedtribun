Rich Tettemer Anchor

Apart from WWLP, Rich has also worked for  KODE-TV in Joplin, Missouri, as the weekend sports anchor and reporter. He also worked for WFLA-TV in Florida as a sports editor. In his career at 22News, Tettemer was voted by the readers of the Valley Advocate and the Springfield Advocate as Best Sportscaster. Tettemer has also earned 13 Associated Press Awards from 1994 through 2006. Since 1989, he has served as co-host of the Children’s Miracle Network Telethon.

Rich Tettemer Education

Tettemer went to the University of South Florida in Tampa and graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Mass Communications and Broadcasting.
