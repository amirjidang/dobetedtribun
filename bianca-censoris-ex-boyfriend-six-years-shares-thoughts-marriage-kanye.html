Bianca Censoris Ex-Boyfriend Of Six Years Shares His Real Thoughts On Her Marriage To Kanye

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Highlights

  • Bianca Censori's ex supports her marriage with Kanye, despite controversies surrounding their relationship, including public indecency reports.
  • Reports suggest Bianca may want a break from Kanye due to feeling overwhelmed by his constant need for attention and promotion.
  • Despite challenges, Bianca and Kanye remain supportive of each other, with Bianca focusing on plans for children post-album release.

There wasn’t a lot known about Bianca Censori before she started making headlines for her relationship with Kanye West. Originating from Australia, she was a Yeezy employee for years before they became romantically involved. But now, someone from Bianca’s past is speaking out for the first time about what she’s like – her ex-boyfriend.

Nick Forgiane dated the architectural designer for six years before she got married to Ye. They first met at the age of 14 in 2007, but didn’t date until 2014 when they were 21 before parting ways in 2020.

Bianca’s Ex Shared His Real Thoughts On Her Romance With Kanye

“Bianca has always been bigger than Melbourne and we both knew this. One time we traveled to America and she told me she would live there one day,” Nick said while opening up to Nova FM’s Fitzy & Wippa.

Nick went on to express support for Bianca and Kanye’s marriage, despite the various rumors circulating about how the rapper treats his wife.

“The girl is extremely talented and took the leap to go chase her dream of being a famous architect, which she’s achieved. Of course, I support them. If Bianca is happy with Kanye, I am happy for her,” Forgiane said. “She has always been supportive of my relationships. I wish them the best and hope it’s a modern-day love story.”

Bianca and Kanye married in December 2022, a month after he finalized his divorce from Kim Kardashian. However, the pair have faced various controversies, largely due to the provocative outfits Bianca has worn while traveling the globe, which are allegedly chosen by Kanye.

At one point, there was demand in Italy for the couple to be fined for public indecency. Later, a police investigation was launched after the couple were caught in seemingly inappropriate behavior riding a gondola in Venice.

Various Reports Claim Kanye And Bianca’s Marriage Is In Trouble

Previous reports claimed Bianca’s family staged an intervention, and that her dad was specifically concerned about Kanye’s influence. However, one of Bianca’s sisters recently went on record to refute the claims and express support for the couple.

While some reports suggest Bianca and Kanye are trying to get pregnant, others claim the architectural designer wants a break from Ye. “She needs a break and needs to step back,” a source told Daily Mail. “She loved the attention but it is getting overwhelming and Kanye keeps pushing and pushing for more, more, more leading up to the release of Vultures 2.”
