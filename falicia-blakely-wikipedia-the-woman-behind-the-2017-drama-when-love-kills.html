Falicia Blakely Age: How Old Is She?

As per Essence, Falicia Blakely’s age is 34 years old. She was born in 1983 to her mother, Betty Blakely, as per her book. And she was 19 years old in August 2002 when the killings were committed. 

In the early days, Blakely lived with her mom in Peach State, Atlanta and didn’t go to school but went to strip to make money. Meanwhile, she was given three consecutive life sentences without the chance of parole. 

Prosecutors had tried to get a death penalty in the case, but her life was limited in exchange for the rest of her life behind bars. Now, Falicia is serving her sentence at Arrendale State Prison.
