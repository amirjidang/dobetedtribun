Is Caroline Stanbury Pregnant? Details About The RHOD Star

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Caroline Stanbury, age 46, is not pregnant, however, her husband Sergio José Carrallo is thinking about using a surrogate.

Following her 2019 divorce from her ex-husband Cem Habib, Caroline Stanbury wed a significantly younger man.

Born and bred in London, Caroline Stanbury now resides in Dubai with her three kids and her new spouse, former Real Madrid soccer star Sergio Carallo.

She is a former stylist who has found success as a reality star and represents some of the most prominent brands in the world.

Stanbury had an appearance in three seasons of “Ladies of London” between 2014 and 2017. Additionally, her shoe line, Black Suede Studio, debuted the previous year.

Is Caroline Stanbury Pregnant With New Husband Sergio José Carrallo Pendás?

Although it has become a sore point, Caroline Stanbury is not pregnant with Sergio José Carrallo’s child. In the fourth episode of season two of The Real Housewives of Dubai, Caroline reportedly shares her thoughts on having another baby.

Despite her fears, she is compelled to become pregnant once more because Sergio does not intend to stay around for very long if she doesn’t.

In the episode, Caroline and Sergio are contemplating their future, including planning to build a new house. When they get together to talk about the design of the house, the topic of family expansion and their contrasting views on it quickly come up.

They explore options, including surrogacy, and their varied viewpoints on the sensitive matter.

Insight On Caroline Stanbury Children And Family

After already having three children with her previous spouse, Caroline Stanbury has not yet started a new family with her current husband Sergio Carrallo.

During her 15 years of marriage to Cem Habib, she gave birth to Yasmin, Zac, and Aaron.

The Real Housewives of Dubai’s cast member once stated that the 18-year age gap between her and her new husband has brought about a set of issues, particularly when it comes to talking about starting a family.

On April 28, Caroline posted a beautiful picture of her family smiling before an arrangement of pink and metallic heart-shaped balloons to celebrate her 46th birthday.
