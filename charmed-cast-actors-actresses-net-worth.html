Every Cast Member Of Charmeds Net Worth

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 9, 2024

  • Holly Marie Combs: $14 Million
  • Rose McGowan: $18 Million

  • Brian Krause: $500K
  • Julian McMahon: $16 Million
  • Kaley Cuoco: $55 Million
  • Drew Fuller: $1.5 Million
  • Dorian Gregory: $2 Million
  • Eric Dane: $7 Million

A Charmed reboot has been released, but nothing will ever live up to the original series that we first fell in love within the 1990s. Starring Alyssa Milano, Holly Marie Combs, Shannen Doherty, and Rose McGowan as the four leads over the course of the series, the show follows the story of the Halliwell sisters—modern-day witches in San Francisco.

Since the end of the show, each cast member has gone on to do different things. They’ve continued to build their acting careers, some of them diving into the world of movies while others explored other genres of television. Check out every cast member of Charmed’s net worth below.

Alyssa Milano: $10 Million

Phoebe Halliwell begins as the youngest of the Charmed sisters, but after losing Prue and finding Paige, she moves into the role of middle sister. With her powers of premonition and empathy, her passionate romances, and her chic style, Phoebe is arguably one of the most popular characters on the show.

Alyssa Milano, who portrayed Phoebe for the show’s eight-season run, has a net worth of approximately $10 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She’s appeared in other acting projects since the end of Charmed and has a strong following on social media.

Holly Marie Combs: $14 Million

Holly Marie Combs played Piper, the middle-turned eldest of the Charmed Ones. Piper’s power is arguably the coolest—who wouldn’t want the ability to freeze time at any awkward moment? Piper’s enduring relationship with Leo also serves as one of the main drivers of the show.

Combs appeared on other TV shows since finishing up on Charmed, including on the drama series Pretty Little Liars where she played Ella Montgomery. She has also attended various Comic-Con events over the years, much to the delight of adoring Charmed fans. These have added to her $14 million net worth.

Rose McGowan: $18 Million

Rose McGowan didn’t come into the Charmed world until it was already a hit show with a sizeable fan base. She first graced our screens in the third season to play Paige Matthews, the long-lost half-sister of Prue, Piper, and Phoebe. Being half a witch and half a whitelighter, Paige has the ability to orb and call for items.

According to Famous Births and Deaths, McGowan has a net worth of $18 million. Other sources state that her net worth actually sits somewhere around $6 million. Either way, she’s doing pretty well!

Shannen Doherty: $10 Million

For the first three seasons of the show, Shannen Doherty played the wise and grounded eldest Halliwell sister, Prue. After the Season Three finale, though, fans never get to see her play Prue again. For reasons that still aren’t entirely clear, Doherty left the show and McGowan came on.

As of 2019, Doherty is worth about $10 million. She has also appeared with her former cast-mate Combs at various Comic-Con events and has starred in other TV shows, including 90210. Doherty is reported to be good friends with Combs, even after all this time.

Brian Krause: $500K

Leo Wyatt is one of those characters that has set all of us up for disappointment. No husband is ever going to be quite as angelic as Leo and that’s been a hard fact to swallow after religiously watching the show for years! Leo appears less in the later seasons due to budgeting issues, but we’re so glad that he returns for the epic finale.

Brian Krause, who portrayed Leo, is said to be worth around $500,000. Like Holly Marie Combs and Shannen Doherty, he has also appeared at Comic-Con events to connect with die-hard Charmed fans.

Julian McMahon: $16 Million

Not to (literally) play the devil’s advocate here, but Julian McMahon is one of the reasons why we fell in love with Charmed. He gives up so much for Phoebe during their relationship, and the only reason he turns evil again in Season Four is because he plots with the Seer to save the Charmed Ones from the Hollow! We love almost all of Phoebe’s boyfriends, but Cole Turner is our favorite.

According to The Washington Note, Julian McMahon has a net worth of $16 million. The Australian-born actor is famous for appearing on Nip/Tuck between 2003 and 2010.

Kaley Cuoco: $55 Million

It isn’t until the last days of Charmed that Kaley Cuoco arrives as Billie, the apprentice witch who first learns from the Charmed Ones before being turned against them by her sister Christie. Those were the days when Cuoco was mostly unknown. Today, she is worth $55 million, according to Cheat Sheet.

Cuoco achieved worldwide fame through her role of Penny on the hit TV show The Big Bang Theory, which came to an end in 2019. We can’t wait to see what other projects she’ll undertake in the future!

Drew Fuller: $1.5 Million

Drew Fuller doesn’t appear in Charmed until the end of the fifth season when his character Chris mysteriously shows up in the manor to help the sisters defeat the titans and save the world. In Season Six, it’s revealed that Chris is actually Piper and Leo’s son—a fact that was written into the script after Holly Marie Combs fell pregnant.

Celebrity Net Worth states that Drew Fuller has a net worth of about $1.5 million. After Charmed, he appeared in several other projects, including Army Wives, and the TV movie Perfect on Paper.

Dorian Gregory: $2 Million

Though he’s just a regular human with no magic powers, Darryl Morris is a strong part of the Charmed family. Over the years, he uses his position as a detective to help the sisters out in endless ways. They wouldn’t have survived without Darryl! Dorian Gregory, who played Darryl, is worth $2 million.

He is most well-known for his role on Charmed, but he has a list of other acting credits under his name. These include the TV series Family Time, and the TV series Without a Trace.

Eric Dane: $7 Million

Eric Dane only appears on the show during Seasons Five and Six, where he plays Phoebe’s boss and boyfriend Jason Dean. Although we will forever worship Phoebe and Cole, we still love Phoebe and Jason together. Their relationship seems to be able to defy all the odds until he finds out that she’s a witch.

Dane is famous for his work on Grey’s Anatomy, as well as for his appearances in movies such as Marley and Me and Valentine’s Day. His net worth is currently listed at $7 million.
