'Ray Donovan': The Cast Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Showtime crime drama series Ray Donovan, cancelled after seven seasons, is a fan favorite. The series follows titular character Ray Donovan through his career and family woes. The popular show is going to conclude with a feature-length film, which is currently being filmed.

Related: Whoopi Goldberg Approves Showtime's Documentary Of Legendary Comedian John Belushi

The star-studded cast's names carry weight in Hollywood, but they have even heavier bank accounts. Here they are ranked by net worth:

12 Eddie Marsan - $800K

British actor Eddie Marsan has had a decades-long career. He's made his money by playing parts in television shows, like popular BBC sitcom Game On, as well as films, including notable movies Hancock, Miami Vice, V for Vendetta, and Sherlock Holmes.

11 Graham Rogers - $800K

Graham Rogers hasn't been acting for long, making his net worth all the more impressive. Rogers had roles in films and multiple episodes of shows like Quantico and Atypical before being promoted to a series regular on Ray Donovan. The young actor can also be found on Netflix's The Kominsky Method.

10 Devon Bagby - $1M

American actor Devon Bagby only had three acting credits before landing his role on Ray Donovan. Bagby most recently starred as Nick in the movie Last Night on Earth. His work has given him an enviable bank account.

9 Katherine Moenning - $2M

Born into a family of performers, actress Katherine Moenning, famed actress Gwyneth Paltrow's cousin, earned her net worth through her work in theatre and film, as well as her starring role in Showtime drama The L Word. Moenning has been reprising her role in The L Word: Generation Q since Ray Donovan ended.

8 Dash Mihok - $3M

Actor and musician Dash Mihok has had a long career in film. His roles in Romeo + Juliet and Silver Linings Playbook, as well as his roles on dozens of television series before landing his starring role in Ray Donovan have earned him a notable net worth. Mihok is also in a band called Diz and the Fam.

7 Pooch Hall - $3.5M

Actor, rapper, and model Pooch Hall's career in entertainment started with small roles in commercials and modeling gigs. He's since amassed his wealth through his starring roles in television show The Game, and as famed boxer Muhammad Ali in biopic Chuck.

6 Paula Malcomson - $4M

Northern Irish actress Paula Malcomson has been acting for nearly thirty years. The veteran performer has earned her money through parts in many films like the wildly successful series The Hunger Games, and as Trixie in television series Deadwood. Malcomson has also appeared in several episodes of both Caprica and Sons of Anarchy. She most recently had a role in an episode of miniseries Watchmen.

5 Kerris Dorsey - $4M

Actress and singer Kerris Dorsey has been performing since she was a child. The star had roles in movies like Walk the Line and Moneyball alongside Hollywood A-listers like Reese Witherspoon and Brad Pitt, as well as parts in television series like Scrubs, Monk, and Mad Men before landing her role as Bridget Donovan in Ray Donovan. Her years of experience have built her a remarkable fortune.

4 Steven Bauer - $5M

3 Liev Schreiber - $40M

Ray Donovan himself, Liev Schreiber has had a prolific career in theater, film, and television. Also a screenwriter, director, and producer, Schreiber has a Tony award and has been nominated for multiple Emmys. Schreiber is also well-known for his vast  narration work in projects like Hard Knocks and 24/7. His illustrious resume has earned him an astounding net worth.

2 Jon Voight - $55M

Jon Voight has been working in Hollywood for decades. The Oscar-winning actor and father of Angelina Jolie has built a an enormous fortune through a long, successful career, becoming known for his roles in several iconic films like Deliverance, Coming Home, Heat, and Pearl Harbor, among others. Until being cast in Ray Donovan, Voight's television work was limited to parts in miniseries and guest-starring roles. Voight is currently filming the cinematic conclusion to Ray Donovan, as well as a dramatization of President Ronald Reagan's life called Reagan.

1 Susan Sarandon - $60M

Another Oscar-winner and seasoned actress on Ray Donovan's cast, Susan Sarandon is known for her roles in The Rocky Horror Picture Show, Thelma & Louise, The Witches of Eastwick, Stepmom, and, most recently, A Bad Moms Christmas. Sarandon has starred in a Broadway production, and has had guest roles on a variety of television series, including Friends and Malcolm in the Middle. Sarandon's work in television has earned her 6 Primetime Emmy nominations, and her work in blockbuster films has earned her an incredible net worth, the highest of the Ray Donovan cast.
