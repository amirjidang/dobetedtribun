Travis Kelces Brother Fuels Engagement Rumors By Calling Taylor Swift A Part Of Their Family

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Highlights

  • Jason Kelce refers to Taylor Swift as part of the "family" during Chiefs' win celebration.
  • Engagement rumors continue to surround Travis and Taylor, with insiders suggesting a secret engagement.
  • Tony Romo mistakenly referred to Taylor as Travis' "wife" during a game, sparking speculation about their relationship status.

Jason Kelce isn’t helping the engagement rumors following Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, as the Chiefs player’s older brother referenced Taylor as part of the “family.”

During the latest episode of the Kelce brothers’ New Heights podcast, Travis referenced the Chiefs big win on Sunday, January 28, which qualified them for the Superbowl. “We had the whole family down on the field. We had me, Mom, Dad and Taylor. Everybody was down there,” Jason explained.

Travis agreed with his brother, saying the “people that mattered” were there to watch him accept the Lamar Hunt Trophy. “Our family, friends and the people that came on the f–king flight with us, dude,” Travis said.

Engagement rumors have bee following Travis and Taylor for months, with some reports claiming he was going to propose during the holidays. Though the couple never announced an engagement, a recent clues surfaced that suggest the couple may simply be keeping it a secret for now.

Taylor And Travis May Be Secretly Engaged

According to Daily Mail, insiders suggest that although the couple's engagement occurred in secrecy, they have decided to delay their announcement until after the Superbowl and Taylor's Eras tour.

The anonymous figure behind the Deux Moi podcast, recognized for unveiling celebrity controversies through both podcast episodes and its Instagram account, purportedly received confirmation from a source about the couple's engagement.

"Taylor and Travis became engaged on 12/31," asserted Deux Moi. "They opted for an intimate moment between just the two of them before attending the party where they were later photographed."

Deux Moi's reference points to a widely circulated video from New Year's Eve capturing the couple in a tender embrace, with speculations from online lip readers suggesting expressions of "I love you" passing between them.

Despite the podcast host's assertion of the accuracy of their information, they indicated that Travis and Taylor are unlikely to officially confirm the news anytime soon. They elaborated, stating, "They don't want to overshadow that. It's too soon."

Travis’ Friends Have Referred to Taylor As His “Wife”

Even more proof that the couple are growing more serious in December came when Travis’ friends referred to Taylor as the NFL star’s “wife.” For example, the Kansas City Chiefs vs. Las Vegas Raiders game on December 25, Tony Romo, serving as a commentator for CBS, once again alluded to Taylor as the NFL star's "wife" rather than his girlfriend. Taylor, present in the stands to support her boyfriend's team, brought along her parents to share in the Christmas Day game.

As Romo highlighted Travis' 900th career reception, the camera swiftly focused on Taylor, enthusiastically cheering him on. Romo's comment, "And his wife loves it — I mean girlfriend," quickly followed, sparking discussions among fans.

This slip wasn't the first time Romo referred to Taylor as Travis' "wife." During the December 10th game, Romo, a former Dallas Cowboys player, made a similar mistake when Taylor was in attendance. "As you see, Kelce’s wife, Taylor Swift, in the audience," Romo initially remarked when cameras captured Swift in the crowd. He promptly corrected himself, saying, "I’m sorry, girlfriend."

Romo's reference to Swift as Kelce's "wife" has ignited speculation among online observers, with many pondering whether there's a possibility the pair secretly tied the knot or got engaged. Alternatively, it could simply be a well-timed PR maneuver on Romo's part, given the considerable attention it has garnered, or perhaps just a genuine mistake. Whatever is the case, it appears Taylor and Travis are down to keep fans guessing.
