Elise Finch husband: Who is Graig Henriques?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Unfortunately, at the age of 51, Elise Finch, a renowned meteorologist for CBS New York, passed away in a sad accident. She passed unexpectedly at a nearby hospital in New York on Sunday evening, and the news of her terrible death was announced shortly after. Her specific cause of death is still a mystery.

Elise, also known as Elise Dione Finch Henriques, joined the WCBS staff in 2007 as a weekend meteorologist and worked there for an incredible 16 years. She had most recently worked as a morning news anchor, joining the 9 a.m. newscast in September of the previous year.

CBS expressed tremendous regret in an official statement, describing Elise as an incredibly accomplished and committed professional who approached her work with great attention. They also recognized her tremendous contributions to the community, notably in Mount Vernon, her hometown.

Above all, Elise was renowned for being a loving and devoted mother to her daughter Grace, as well as a supporting wife to her husband Graig Henriques, a photojournalist at WCBS.

Jessica Moore of WCBS became obviously emotional as she honored her good friend and colleague during a live televised tribute. The WCBS crew conveyed their heartfelt condolences and support to Elise’s daughter Grace and her husband Graig during this difficult time.

“Elise was a gifted and consummate professional who took great care with her work. She was also a wonderful ambassador in the community, including her hometown of Mount Vernon.

Above all, Elise was a fiercely loving and devoted mother to her daughter Grace and a wife to Graig Henriques, who is a photojournalist at WCBS.”
