If you’ve been meaning to update your collection of handbags, then the countdown is on until August 5 to take advantage of the Nordstrom Anniversary Sale and its deals on designer purses!

The Tory Burch Small Marsden Leather Tote is one of the luxury items up for grabs and it’s a great option if you’re looking for a staple accessory that elevates casual looks and complements dressy attire.

With a boxy design made from supple pebbled leather and a polished chain-link hardware, this is a classy handbag built to last. An extra crossbody strap and separate zip compartments make the tote a sophisticated, practical choice.

Order this beauty in the classic black and gold combination or opt for the equally stylish light oak color. The soft cream shade can also look stunning when worn with both neutrals and bold shades.

Styling possibilities are pretty endless and this is a bag that pairs well with outfits for any season. The tote is available at the sale price of $322.90, which is down from the original $498 tag.

Grab this designer bargain in your preferred shade for over $100 off while it’s still in stock!
