What Happened Between North West And Jessica Simpson's Daughter, Maxwell Johnson

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 4, 2024

It's no secret the internet is obsessed with North West. We are constantly making her TikTok and fashion week appearances go viral. Even her rare singing moments.

The norisblackbook Instagram account that makes fun of the Kardashians as North West, which has 1.6 million followers, solidifies how much the internet cares about Miss West.

So, of course, people are wondering who makes the cut to be a part of North's friend group. From lavish birthday trips to a mansion that dreams are made of, we imagine the list of those wishing for the honor to be called her friend is quite long.

One person who we know can call herself North's friend is Jessica Simpson's daughter Maxwell Johnson. This girl group seems to have the best time when together, and boy, do they know how to throw a party that will make anyone jealous for not getting the invite.

Are Maxwell Johnson And North West Friends?

Maxwell Johnson is the oldest of Jessica Simpson and Eric Johnson's children. The couple has three children: Maxwell, age ten; Ace, age nine; and Birdie, age three.

Maxwell recently celebrated her tenth birthday, and she did it in style. Her birthday bash was outdoors, with North in attendance, and Penelope Disick, the daughter of Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick. The girls seemed to be all smiles and surrounded by plush toys.

According to Simpson, the two girls met while playing on the same basketball team, coached by Eric Johnson, and immediately hit it off. While speaking to US Weekly, Simpson said , "One of Maxwell's best friends is North."

She continued, "And so, watching North, she is amazing, she is a great kid, and she will be a part of the change in this world. Her and Maxwell alone."

With the two seemingly thick as thieves, it only makes sense that when it was North's birthday, the invite was sent to Maxwell. Only Miss West isn't playing around when it comes to celebrating.

North West Knows How To Throw A Party

Fans were able to catch a glimpse into what it's like to attend a celebrity kid's party when Kim Kardashian took to social media and posted a slew of photos showing off North's birthday bash.

The official party was titled "Camp North" and kicked off with six of North's friends meeting at a hangar and jumping onto a private plane. The plane was decked out in nature decor with pillows shaped like logs and translucent-like material to reflect clouds.

Once the girls arrived at their destination, it was nonstop outdoor fun, including a ride on a river raft, a hike across a footbridge, and zip lining above the trees. They also all received matching print pajamas.

And when the day was over, they each got individual indoor tents decorated with faux fluffy deer heads and fake blood splatter. A private wilderness experience for a ninth birthday? North knows how to do luxury.
