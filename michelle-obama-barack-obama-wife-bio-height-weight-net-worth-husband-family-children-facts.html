Michelle Obama is an American Former First Lady of the United States of America. She is better known as the wife of Barack Obama. He is the 44th President of the United States of America, in 1992. She took a leading role in the “Let’s Move!” initiative, which attempts to fight against childhood obesity, and has been a strong advocate for the rights of veterans and the LGBT community. Tune in bio and know more about Michelle Obama’s age, height, weight, net worth, career, family and many more facts about her.

Michelle Obama Bio, Age & Family

How old is Michelle Obama? Her birthday is on January 17, 1964. At present, she is 56-yrs old. She holds American nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity. She was the first African-American first lady. She was raised on the South Side of Chicago, Illinois. Her father suffered from multiple sclerosis. As of educational qualifications, Obama is a graduate of Princeton University and Harvard Law School.

Michelle Obama Height & Weight

How tall is Michelle Obama? She stands at a height of 5 ft 8 in tall or else 1.80 m or 180 cm. She weighs around 57 Kg or 127 lbs. Her figure stats are 34-27-37 inches. She wears a bra cup size of 33 D. She has brown eyes and hair.
