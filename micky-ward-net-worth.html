Micky Ward’s Retirement and Comeback

With his renewed focus and determination, Ward returned to the sport he loved. He quickly proved that he still had what it took to be a champion, winning his first nine fights after his comeback. This remarkable run culminated in him becoming the WBU Intercontinental Light Welterweight Champion.
