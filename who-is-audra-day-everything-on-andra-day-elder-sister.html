Who Is Audra Day? Everything On Andra Day Elder Sister

Audra Day is reportedly the elder sister of Andra Day, a renowned American singer, songwriter, and actress. She has received different accolades, including a Golden Globe and Grammy Award.

Who Is Audra Day?

American singer Andra Day’s big sister’s name is reportedly Audra Day. The renowned singer hit the 2021 Academy Awards red carpet last year with a special someone by her side: her elder sister!

The first-time Oscar nominee said she was always planning to bring her loving sister as her date to the Awards. Andra even stated her family had battled for the spot alongside her at the Academy Awards.

She chose her sister as her date, despite the rest of her family trying to take the spot. Andra joked the battle just became a way for her to get free stuff and gist from her family.

Ahead of the Oscars, where the singer was nominated for her remarkable performance as the titular music icon in The United States vs. Billie Holiday, she spoke about her experience working on the film.

Andra Day Sister Audra Day Age – Wiki Bio

Audra Day is probably in her late 30s or early 40s, given that her younger sister Andra Day is thirty-seven years old.

Andra was born on December 30, 1984, in Edmonds, Washington, U.S.

The actress moved to Southern California at age three with her family and grew up in Southeast San Diego, California. Her passion for singing was seen from an early age, when she began singing at the Forst United Methodist Church in Chula Vista, California.

Andra started taking dance lessons when she was five and later attended Valencia Park Elementary School. She credits the school for fostering her interest in performing arts. At age 12, Andra was introduced to the sounds of jazz vocalists such as Ella Fitzgerald and Billie Holiday.
